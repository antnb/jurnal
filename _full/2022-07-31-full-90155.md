The purpose of this study was to find out the description of Generic SIKDA at Puskesmas Kediri I. This study used a qualitative method. Informants from this study came from the Tabanan District Health Office and Kediri I Health Center with a total of 11 informants selected through purposive sampling technique. Data collection was carried out using in-depth interview guidelines and observation sheets. Data analysis was carried out by collecting data"
