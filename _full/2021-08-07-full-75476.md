  - "Putusan Pengadilan ABSTRACT The purpose of writing this scientific article"
  - "such as; (1) to find out and analyze the juridical implications of unlawful acts in cases of ownership of land rights by unlawful acts based on Court Decision Number 96/PDT.G/2019/PN.Amp. (2) to find out and analyze the form of legal liability for unlawful acts in the case of ownership of rights to property based on Court Decision Number 96/PDT.G/2019/PN.Amp. This article is classified as an empirical legal research that uses a fact approach. The primary data in this article was obtained through interview techniques with informants at the Amlapura District Court. Based on the results of the analysis"
  - "it is known that the juridical implication of an unlawful act in the case of ownership of land rights is that the defendant loses the right to ownership of the land which is the object of the dispute. The form of legal liability from the defendant for the unlawful act in the case of ownership of the right to property is to provide material and non-material compensation to the plaintiff based on a court decision.
