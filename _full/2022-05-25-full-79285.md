  - "Arisan Online ABSTRACT The purpose of this study is to determine the validity of the online social gathering as stated in the ITE Law and to determine the settlement process if there is a default in the online social gathering. The method used is a normative legal research method. The legal materials used include: primary legal materials and secondary legal materials. The types of approaches used are: a. regulatory approach; b. conceptual approach; and c. argumentative approach. The validity of the online arisan agreement carried out by the administrator with the online arisan participant is said to be valid because the agreement is considered valid if it is in accordance with the provisions of Article 1320 of the Civil Code and the Electronic Law. The dispute resolution process in this online social gathering can be done through a court process or out of court.
