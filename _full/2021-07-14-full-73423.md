  - "First to find out the conformity between Article 359 paragraph (2) of Undang-Undang No. 1 Tahun 2009 (Aviation Law) with human rights in Indonesian constitution. Second"
  - "analyzing the appropriate construction norms to limiting information on the results of aircraft accident investigations in Indonesia. The research in this paper uses the normative legal method with a law approach"
  - "a concept approach"
  - "and a case approach. The results of the study show that Article 359 paragraph (2) of Aviation Law is contrary to constitution (unconstitutional). This is based on three things. First"
  - "there is a new norm in the explanation of the article. Second"
  - "article a quo doesn’t contain a time to limiting the information which is a human right. Third"
  - "based on the proportionality test"
  - "Article a quo doesn’t appropriate the requirements of Conformity Stage because the restrictions imposed by the state"
  - "don’t appropriate the Article 28J of the UUD 1945"
  - "Article 17 of Undang-Undang No. 14 Tahun 2008 (Public Information Openness Law)"
  - "and the Siracusa Principle. Article 359 paragraph (2) of the Aviation Law doesn’t fulfill the principle of fair legal certainty if the norm doesn’t change. Aircraft transportation modes increasing the pace of the economy"
  - "socio-culture"
  - "politics"
  - "and security-defense"
  - "that is makes Article 359 paragraph (2) of Aviation Law very important to updated. It is the government's responsibility to ensure the enforcement of human rights.
