  - "the authors collect and analyze library materials obtained from primary data and secondary data. The results of the study show that architects get protection that their work cannot be used without the author's permission. Architects who experience loss of economic rights due to copyright infringement are entitled to compensation (regulated in Article 96 UUHC)"
  - "by filed a civil suit to the “Commercial Court” (Article 100 first paragraph of UUHC). One of the main factors in the difficulty of enforcing copyright infringement of landscape architecture works is the lack of socialization about the importance of registering creations for architects.
