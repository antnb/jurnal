  - "namely the Directorate General of Intellectual Property Rights or which can be abbreviated as the Director General of IPR. Judging from the use of methods in this study"
  - "namely using normative legal research methods using a statutory approach. This study produces a theory that the work of children's songs that have not passed the registration process at the Directorate General of IPR can be legally protected on the basis of exclusive rights obtained when the work has been applied as a medium of learning and supported based on the basis of protection that is closely related to copyright in the country of Indonesia based on Act No. 28 Year 2014 on Copyright"
