Bali beef is an ideal medium for the growth of microorganisms because it is rich in protein minerals and water When placed at room temperature the meat will spoil more quickly The purpose of this study was to determine the effect of laying beef at room temperature on the pH value and total bacteria This research method tested 24 pieces of beef with a weight of 10 grams each and placed at room temperature 2730C then tested with pH and total bacteria tests
