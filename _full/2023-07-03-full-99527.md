This study is an exploratory research with the aim of determining the physical characteristics and storage of raw materials for Healing Tea herbal tea at PT Karsa Abadi The raw materials of this herbal tea are gemitir flowers knob flowers rose flowers and citronella leaves The research methods used are the RHS color chart in the raw material inventory process sortation the gravimetric method for checking moisture content in the raw material drying process
