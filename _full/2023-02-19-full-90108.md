The research location at PT. Bali Regional Development Bank. First of all"
  - "the results of this study"

  - "(2) comparison"
  - "(3) substantive in which there are a number of clauses that are conditions for granting bank loans. Second"
  - "the legal force of an underhand credit contract has permanent legal force as long as it is not denied by the debtor. If the credit contract/credit agreement is denied by the debtor"
