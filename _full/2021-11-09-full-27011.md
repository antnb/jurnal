<h2><a name="bookmark4"></a><span class="font3" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark5"></a>Abstrak</span></h2>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Skripsi ini berjudul “Kata Majemuk pada Website Berita BBC”. Penelitian ini bertujuan untuk mengidentifikasi dan mengklasifikasi jenis-jenis kata majemuk pada Website Berita BBC dan juga menentukan makna dari kata majemuk yang muncul pada Website Berita BBC. Sumber data penelitian ini diambil dari Website Berita BBC, sebuah website yang memungkinkan para pengguna untuk mendapatkan berita aktual, video, audio, maupun artikel-artikel lainnya. Metoda yang digunakan dalam pengumpulan data adalah metoda dokumentasi dengan teknik pencatatan. Metoda deskriptif kwalitatif digunakan dalam menganalisa data yang telah terkumpul.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Teori yang digunakan untuk menganalisa data adalah teori kata majemuk oleh Plag (2003) dan Lieber (2009). Makna kata majemuk ditentukan dengan menggunakan teori makna oleh Palmer (1984). Berdasarkan teori kata majemuk oleh Plag (2003), hasil penelitian ini menunjukkan bahwa terdapat empat jenis kata majemuk yang ditemukan pada Webiste Berita BBC, yaitu nominal compound, adjectival compound, verbal compound, dan neoclassical compound. Selain itu, terdapat dua jenis makna yang ditemukan pada analisa data, yaitu makna transparan dan makna opaque.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h2><a name="bookmark6"></a><span class="font3" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark7"></a>1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Background of the Study</span></h2></li></ul>
<p><span class="font3">In this era, globalization becomes a global phenomenon that cannot be avoided by the society. Globalization is considered a global phenomenon that makes everything seem easier and simpler. One of the benefits that has been caused by globalization can be seen from the aspect of communication and information. People that live in different countries are able to know any</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">information about what is happening in other countries at the same time. The spread of information becomes fast and faster and it seems like there is no border between one country and another. People can get the information or news just by sitting in the house and watching television, listening to the radio, or reading news on magazines or newspapers. By developing of technologies, news is not only presented on printed out papers but also there is a digital way of getting different types of news from countries around of the world. News which is not printed out on paper and can be read through the internet is called digital news.</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">Digital news has brought easier way for people to get various news every time and everywhere they want to. One of the digital news websites is BBC (British Broadcasting Corporation) which is the public service broadcaster of the United Kingdom. This news website contains a lot of news that can be read by people around the world. The news texts on this website contain new words that are related to word formation, especially compounding. According to Plag (2003), compounds are the combination of two words to form a new word. Compounding is the most productive type of word formation process in English as stated also by Plag (2003).</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">The creations of compound words depend on the creativity that language users have chosen. Compounding process is not only about creating news words, but also creating new meaning. Plag (2003) stated that compounding has four types such as nominal compounds, adjectival compounds, verbal compounds, and neo-classical compounds. Compound words can be found in the news texts of BBC website.</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">This present study discusses the types of compound words that occur in BBC news website. Besides, this study also discusses the meaning of compound nouns which is considered important since it might have different new meaning after the word is combined with other word.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>

<h2><a name="bookmark12"></a><span class="font3" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark13"></a>4. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Research Method</span></h2></li></ul>
<p><span class="font3">Documentation method was used to collect the data in this study which means that the data were taken from written material, news texts in BBC News Website. Meanwhile, descriptive qualitative method was used to analyze the data in this study which means that the information gathered is not in numerical form. Qualitative method was applied to analyzing data in order to give a deep explanation descriptively. Descriptive qualitative method was used to classify the types of compound words based on the theory by Plag (2003) and Lieber (2009) and to analyze the meaning of compound words based on the theory of meaning by Palmer (1984).</span></p>

<p><span class="font3">1.</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;"> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Black market</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font3">The word </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻblackʼ</span><span class="font3"> has lexical meaning </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻthe very darkest color owing to the absence of or complete absorption of light; the opposite of whiteʼ</span><span class="font3"> whereas the</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">word </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻmarketʼ</span><span class="font3"> has lexical meaning ‘</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">a regular gathering of people for the purchase and sale of provisions, livestock, and other commoditiesʼ</span><span class="font3">. However, when the word </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻblackʼ</span><span class="font3"> and ‘</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">marketʼ</span><span class="font3"> are grouped into one, it produces a new meaning. The meaning cannot be transparently identified from its constituent parts (opaque meaning). It does not mean that compound word </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻblack marketʼ</span><span class="font3"> is a kind of market or a market which is dark or market with the absence of the light. The word </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻblack marketʼ</span><span class="font3"> means </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻan illegal traffic or trade in officially controlled or scarce commoditiesʼ.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font3">2.</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;"> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Cameraman</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font3">Semantically, the word </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻcameraʼ</span><span class="font3"> has lexical </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">meaning ʻa device for recording visual images in the form of photographs, film, or video signalsʼ </span><span class="font3">whereas the word </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻmanʼ</span><span class="font3"> has lexical meaning </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻan adult human maleʼ</span><span class="font3">. When the word </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻcameraʼ</span><span class="font3"> and </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻmanʼ</span><span class="font3"> are grouped into a new word ‘</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">cameraman</span><span class="font3">’, the meaning can be determined from the meaning of its parts (transparent meaning). It can be identified that the word </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻcameramanʼ</span><span class="font3"> means ‘</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">an adult human male which uses a camera, a device for recording visual images in the form of photographsʼ</span><span class="font3">. Furthermore, the word </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻmanʼ</span><span class="font3"> has another meaning ‘</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">a male person associated with a particular place, activity, or occupation</span><span class="font3">’. The compound word </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻcameramanʼ </span><span class="font3">means ‘</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">a person whose profession is operating a video, television, or film camera</span><span class="font3">’</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">.</span></p>

<p><span class="font3">1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Internet</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font3">The word </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">‘inter’</span><span class="font3"> comes from lexeme of Latin origin ‘</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">h</span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">i</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">enter’</span><span class="font3"> (Lewis and Shirt, 1879) and has meaning ‘</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">between; from one to another</span><span class="font3">’ whereas the word </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻnetʼ</span><span class="font3"> has lexical meaning ‘</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">a type of material that is made of string, thread or wire twisted or tied together, with small spaces in betweenʼ</span><span class="font3">. However, when the word </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻinterʼ</span><span class="font3"> and ‘</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">netʼ</span><span class="font3"> are grouped into one, it produces a new meaning. The meaning cannot be transparently identified from its constituent parts (opaque meaning). It does not mean that compound word </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻinternetʼ</span><span class="font3"> is ‘between materials that are made of strin</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">gʼ</span><span class="font3"> or </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻsharing the net from one to anotherʼ</span><span class="font3">. The word </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻinternetʼ</span><span class="font3"> has meaning </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻan international computer network connecting other networks and computers from companies, universities, and so onʼ.</span></p>

<p><span class="font3">2. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Telegram</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font3">The word </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻteleʼ</span><span class="font3"> comes from lexeme of Greek origin </span><a href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%cf%84%e1%bf%86%ce%bb%ce%b5%23Ancient_Greek"><span class="font3">‘</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">τῆλε</span></a><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʼ (têle)</span><span class="font3"> (Liddell &amp;&nbsp;Scott, 1940) and has meaning ‘over a long distance; </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">farʼ</span><span class="font3"> whereas the word </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻgramʼ</span><span class="font3"> has lexical meaning ‘</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">a unit for measuring weightʼ</span><span class="font3">. However, when the word </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻteleʼ</span><span class="font3"> and ‘</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">gramʼ</span><span class="font3"> are grouped into one, it produces a new meaning. The meaning cannot be transparently identified from its constituent parts (opaque meaning). It does not mean that compound word </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻtelegramʼ</span><span class="font3"> is a unit for measuring weight that is far. The word </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻtelegramʼ</span><span class="font3"> means </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">ʻa message sent by telegraphʼ.</span></p>
