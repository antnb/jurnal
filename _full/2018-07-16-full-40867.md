  - "GC-MS ABSTRACT: Optimization of compound detection of AB-Fubinaca and 5-Fluoro ADB has been done using GC-MS instrument. The purposes these optimization were to obtain the most suitable solvent for extracting both of the substances and to reduce the analysis time of the two compounds. The solvents used in the extraction were acetone and methanol. The optimization of analysis time was achieved by varying the flow rate of the carrier gas"
  - "the injection temperature"
  - "and column programmed temperature. The solvent selection results show that the most suitable solvent for the extraction process of AB-Fubinaca is acetone while for the 5-Fluoro ADB is methanol. The time efficiency analyzes of AB-Fubinaca and 5-Fluoro ADB compounds extracted with acetone and methanol respectively were 75% and 63%"
  - "which were obtained on injection temperature"
  - "flow rate and column programmed temperature of 2900C"
  - "1.5 ml/minute and column programmed temperature III which is started with initial temperature of 800C and increased of 300C/minute to 2900C. The results of this study show that the increasing of injection temperature was directly proportional to the time efficiency of the analysis. The time for analysing the sampel was shorten from 30 minutes to 10 minutes."
