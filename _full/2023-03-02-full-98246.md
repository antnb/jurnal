This study aims to determine the prevalence and risk factors for trypanosomiasis in horses in East Sumba The samples used were blood from 286 horses taken in the West and East Regions of East Sumba Regency Blood sampling was carried out through the jugular vein Diagnosis of trypanosomiasis is made by examining thin blood smears stained with 10 Giemsa The risk factors for trypanosomiasis were studied by distributing specially designed questionnaires to farmers
