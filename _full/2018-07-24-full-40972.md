<h2><a name="bookmark0"></a><span class="font4"><a name="bookmark1"></a>The Helping Behavior of Senior and Vocational High School Students Based on the Values of Pancasila in Pemalang Regency</span></h2>
<h4><a name="bookmark2"></a><span class="font3"><a name="bookmark3"></a>Dedit Priyono, Alvanov Z Mansoor, Intan R Mutiaz</span></h4>

<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Abstract</span><span class="font2">The decline in a social relationship has led people, particularly teenagers, to an unwillingness to help others. Therefore, there needs such an effort to help teenagers realize the importance of helping others. One possible effort is to hold a character building program. In this case, the writer involves </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pancasila</span><span class="font2"> as the fundamental principle of the program to relate its values to the helping behavior the teenagers might show. Accordingly, this study focuses on finding the helping behavior related to the values contained in the </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Sila</span><span class="font2"> of </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pancasila</span><span class="font2"> shown by the teenagers. This descriptive-qualitative study uses Focus Group Discussion (FGD) and literature study methods to collect data. From the research, the writer finds </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Sila 2 Kemanusiaan Yang Adil dan Beradab</span><span class="font2"> fits the helping behavior shown by the teenagers the most. The helping behavior shown relates, particularly, with the following values of </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pancasila,</span><span class="font2"> i.e., &quot;To recognize equality both in rights and responsibility, to uphold the values of humanity,&quot; and &quot;To promote fairness to others.&quot; Nevertheless, teenagers have not shown yet the one related with &quot;To practice tolerance and show empathy.&quot;</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">Keywords: Focus Group Discussion (FGD), Pancasila, Teenagers, Helping Behavior.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">I. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;INTRODUCTION</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The culture of helping others has been part of Indonesian people. The habit of helping others has been implemented by Indonesian people from one generation to another one. In line with Ibnu Khaldun in Saragih (2015) that living together and helping each is considered human core need. This need has to be fulfilled so as to keep humanity exists. To make it happen, collectivismshould be involved for the sake of forming social order in which people cannot live alone. Accordingly, people live together in a mutual relationship where they can share each other to fulfill their need of life.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">The helping behavior is a crucial thing as people are social beings in nature. They will never be able to live alone. People need others since they were born. This is in line with Sears in Gigy (2014) who clarified that &quot;people are not individual beings who can live alone, yet social beings who really need others.&quot; The helping behavior means the willingness to help others sincerely. In other words, according to Santrock in Waas (2015), “helping behavior is prioritizing other who need help rather than prioritizing personal interests.&quot; Helping behavior should have been owned by human, and it needs to be taught to humans since they were kids.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">Puberty, as Hurlockin Herlina (2016) stated, is subdivided into two, i.e., immature(11/12-16/17years old) and mature(16/17-18years old).Individually, teenagers reach their maturity during their late adolescence. Krori in Herlina (2016) added that teenage is a crucial period of life, which covers the period of</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">transition, the period of change, the period of which teenagers are in the age of trouble, the period in which teenagers search for their self-identities, the period of dreadness, the period of unrealism, and the period of threshold towards maturity.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">Nowadays, the interaction among people has shifted as a result of the era of globalization and modernization. It is followed by the change of the values of social life both quantitatively and qualitatively. People tend to be selfish. The decline of willingness to help others indicates this kind of phenomenon. It happens within societies, especially teenagers. The helping behavior among teenagers starts to be decreasing. It significantly increases the individualism among teenagers. Besides, the teenagers' lifestyle, which is hedonistic, causes the teenagers to be egoistic and ignorant towards the condition of others. They have been individualistic ones and tend to be aggressive by showing off violence and others juvenile crimes.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">The fact that teenagers tend to be aggressive can be known from the news reported by media like television and newspaper. For instance, there had ever been news about the death of supporters in a football match between PERSIB versus PERSIJA. As cited in </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Kompas</span><span class="font2"> online, the case happened for one of the PERSIB supporters was seriously injured after being attacked by the supporters of PERSIJA. The injury caused the supporter of PERSIB to die. The detailed chronology can be accessed in</span><a href="http://regional.kompas.com/read/2017/07/28/07535911/kronologi-pengeroyokan-ricko-bobotoh-persib-yang-meninggal-dunia"><span class="font2">(</span><span class="font2" style="text-decoration:underline;">http://regional.kompas.com/read/2017/07/28/07535911</span></a><span class="font2" style="text-decoration:underline;"> </span><a href="http://regional.kompas.com/read/2017/07/28/07535911/kronologi-pengeroyokan-ricko-bobotoh-persib-yang-meninggal-dunia"><span class="font2" style="text-decoration:underline;">/kronologi-pengeroyokan-ricko-bobotoh-persib-yang-</span></a><span class="font2" style="text-decoration:underline;"></span><a href="http://regional.kompas.com/read/2017/07/28/07535911/kronologi-pengeroyokan-ricko-bobotoh-persib-yang-meninggal-dunia"><span class="font2" style="text-decoration:underline;">meninggal-dunia</span><span class="font2">)</span></a><span class="font2">. Besides, the video of the case was widely spread.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">Another news about the juvenile crime was the fight among female teenagers uploaded via youtube by weka animal lovers (2016), a youtuber account, named “</span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">cewek Bandung berkelahi sampe buka bukaan</span><span class="font2">.&quot; The video was watched by more than one million viewers. Ironically, the fight was watched and even recorded by their peers.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">Those two examples draw a conclusion that it is a necessity to build the teenagers' character based on a strong principle. It is considered so in order to guide teenagers to behave properly in their social lives. With regard to that idea, </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pancasila</span><span class="font2">, as the ideology of the country, is then considered right as the basic principle in building the characters of teenagers. It is because </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pancasila</span><span class="font2"> has values, which are in line with the effort of building the characters of teenagers in that nationalism based characters is set as the goal. The values are included in each </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">sila</span><span class="font2"> of </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pancasila</span><span class="font2">. The values of </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pancasila</span><span class="font2"> are deemed as norms regulating how people live their lives.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">According to Moedjantoin Wahana (1993), the values of </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pancasila</span><span class="font2"> has the lure that attracts peopleto carry them out. He stated also that the values are considered as a goal, which motivates the Indonesian citizen to behave and do something. The importance of the values of </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pancasila</span><span class="font2"> had been formulated by the founders of Indonesia.The formulation was based on the consideration that the values of </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pancasila</span><span class="font2"> are in line with the ideal way of life of Indonesia.Therefore, as the Indonesian citizen, we must uphold them in our lives.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">Based on the brief explanation above, the aims of the research are as follows: (i) to define the helping behavior shown by teenagers in Pemalang Regency, (ii) to find out the forms of the helping behavior known by teenagers according to the values of </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pancasila</span><span class="font2">.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">II. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Method</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">This descriptive-qualitative research used Focus Grup Discussion (FGD) and the study of literature. Focus Group Discussion (FGD) was done twice. The first one was done at the terrace of the mosque of SMK PGRI 2 Taman with the ten school activists. It aimed to find the teen communities in Pemalang Regency.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">The second Focus Group Discussion (FGD) was done at the same place with different participants. There were eight representatives of the teen communities, such as: motor club, urbex people, skateboard freestyler, </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pecinta Goyang Dangdut Koplo</span><span class="font2"> (PGDK), </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Bis Komunity</span><span class="font2">, Muppets (</span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Komunitas Pecinta Pets Pemalang</span><span class="font2">), </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Syechermania</span><span class="font2">, and </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pecinta Grup Al-munsidin</span><span class="font2">. The discussion was aimed to find out the forms of the helping behavior the teenagers know.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">The discussion and interview on the helping behavior among teenagers were done to find out the helping behavior related to the values of </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pancasila.</span><span class="font2"> The values of </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pancasila</span><span class="font2"> addressed are the ones taken from the book of </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">UUD 1945</span><span class="font2"> Amendment I,II,III, and IV, which is published by the team of Palito Media (2016). The values addressed are those reflecting the helping behavior.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">Focus Group Discussion (FGD) was done to find out the teenage communities in </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pemalang</span><span class="font2"> regency and the</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">helping behavior known by the teenagers. The literature study was used to find out the helping behavior available in the values of </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pancasila</span><span class="font2">.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">In the </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Sila Ketuhanan Yang Maha Esa</span><span class="font2">, there are 2 out of 7 values, which address the helping behavior i.e., 1) To develop tolerance towards the practice of worship according to the individual belief, and 2) Not to impose others on the individual belief.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">In the </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Sila Kemanusiaan yang adil dan beradab</span><span class="font2">, there are 6 out of 10 values, which address the helping behavior, i.e.: 1) To recognize the equality in self-dignity and human rights regardless of the tribes, ancestry, religion, beliefs, sex, social status, skin color, etc., 2) To develop the loving behaviour towards others, 3) To</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">develop tolerance and respect towards others, 4) To</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">remind people of the arbitrariness they need to get rid off, and 6) To encorage people to stand on the truth and justice.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">Meanwhile, there is no value addressing the helping behaviour found in the </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Sila Persatuan Indonesia dan Kerakyatan yang dipimpin oleh hikmat kebijaksanaan dalam permusyawaratan perwakilan</span><span class="font2">.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">In the </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">SilaKeadilan sosial bagi seluruh rakyat Indonesia</span><span class="font2">, there are 4 out of 11 values, which address the helping behaviour i.e.: 1) To encourage people to do good things, which reflect togetherness and mutual help, 4) To respect human rights, 5) To encourage the practice of helping others, and 11) To encourage the practice of doing good things for the sake of betterment in life.</span></p>

<p><span class="font2">III. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;FINDINGS AND DISCUSSION</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;">
<li>
<p><span class="font2">III.1.</span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The teenage communities of the Senior and Vocational High Schools in </span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">Pemalang </span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">regency.</span></p></li></ul></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The pre-adolescent development, as Sulaeman (1995) stated, is marked by the increase of interests in personal appearance. Besides, the formation of the peer group and the social activities that follow indicate it so. The preadolescent development is also affected by the previous development, which contributes towards the ability the teenagers may have to communicate and socialize with others. It is in line with Conradi in Sulaeman (1995) who believed that teenagers are able to participate well in social life when they have undergone the pre-adolescent development proportionally.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">In the adolescence period, teenagers are interested more in involving themselves in the teenage groups or communities. It is considered as a common part the teenagers normally undergo. In this case, the communities the teenagers involve themselves in are the ones that address their certain orientation, norms, and values, and the deal they set and is agreed by their group.Next, it is also explained about the group or crowd made by some certain teenagers in that their presence is not only based on the same interests among them, but also on the same need and motivation to do various social activities.</span></p>

<p><span class="font2">III.2. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Helping Behavior Based On the Values of </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pancasila</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The data of the helping behavior based on the values of </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pancasila was</span><span class="font2"> found out from the discussion with one representative from each of the teenage communities in </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pemalang</span><span class="font2"> regency. The discussion and interview were addressed to the eight students of the Vocational School of PGRI 2, </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Taman</span><span class="font2">. Those eight students were from </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">One Look Style (OLS) Zone Pemalang, urbex people, skateboard freestyler, Pasukan Goyang Dangdut Koplo (PGDK), BisMania, Badut Cobra Talenta (Barata), Syechermania,</span><span class="font2"> and </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Al-Munsyider.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">The discussion consisted of three main sections: asking students’ opinion about helping each other, asking students’ opinion about solidarity and the reasons why we</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">need to help others, and asking students about the helping behavior according to the values of </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pancasila</span><span class="font2">.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">In the first section of the discussion, the students representing each of their community agreed that helping behavior is an effort to help others overcome the problemsand face the difficulties they may have. Next, in the second section of the discussion, the students were interviewed. The questions asked was about solidarity and why they need to help others. From the opinion they shared, there were four reasons why they need to help others: 1) empathy, 2) the nature of humans as social beings, 3) religious orders and culture, and 4) sympathy. The last section of the discussion, the participants were asked about the helping behavior related to the values of </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pancasila</span><span class="font2">. It was found that only some values of </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pancasila</span><span class="font2"> addressing the helping behavior. The values were further discussed and the result of the discussion</span></p>

<p><span class="font2">Next, the helping behaviour related to the values included in the </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Sila Kedua Pancasila</span><span class="font2"> i.e., “Kemanusiaan Yang Adil dan Beradab”, are“To recognize the equality in self-dignity and human rights regardless of the tribes, ancestry, religion, beliefs, sex, social status, skin color, etc.” and “ To remind people of the arbitrariness towards others they need to get rid off”. In line with those values, the PGDK community has ever helped their classmate who had ever been bullied by his friends. As well as the Bus dan Pecinta Reptil communities, they both have ever done the same things as the PGDK community did. <p><span class="font2">dan Beradab”: To uphold the values of humanity. The participants were asked to give their opinions about motorists who disobeyed the traffic rules such as stopping at zebra crossing when the traffic light was red and those who passed their vehicles through pavement when traffic jam.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">All participants of the discussion gave the same opinions towards the issue. They thought that the disobedience has done by the motorists obviously deprived the right of others. They said that the motorists should not have done those traffic violations for the sake of allowing the pedestrians to cross easily. They also said that passing the vehicles through the pavements disturbed the pedestrian to walk comfortably. The participants further said that they saw those traffic violations through the TV. They added that they never saw those traffic violations directly around them. <p><span class="font2">IV. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;CONCLUSION</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">From the discussion aforementioned, it can be concluded that most of the participants have known and are able to explain about the helping behavior related to the values of </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pancasila</span><span class="font2">. The values of </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pancasila </span><span class="font2">addressing the helping behaviour the participants of the discussion have known cover “To develop tolerance towards the practice of worship according to the individual belief” (in </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Sila Pertama Pancasila</span><span class="font2">), and “To recognize the equality in self-dignity and human rights regardless of the tribes, ancestry, religion, beliefs, sex, social status, skin color, etc.” and “ To remind people of the arbitrariness towards others they need to get rid off” (in </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Sila Kedua Pancasila</span><span class="font2">).</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">Meanwhile, the value of </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Pancasila</span><span class="font2"> (in </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Sila Kedua</span><span class="font2">), the participants, could not describe was the value &quot;To develop tolerance among others.&quot; The participants only mentioned the examples towards tolerance, i.e., lending their friends sarongs for praying and suggesting motorists to obey the traffic rules.</span></p>
