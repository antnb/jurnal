The research objective is to verify the effect of the adoption of Extensible Business Reporting Language XBRL on information asymmetry in companies listed on the IDX The sample was selected using a purposive sampling technique which was then analyzed using multiple linear regression The research sample consisted of 329 companies with a total sample of 1974 companies being observed The results of this study show that the adoption of XBRL has a positive effect
