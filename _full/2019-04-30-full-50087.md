Sukawana Village is a village patterned in the Bali Aga culture located in the highland region of Kintamani District Bangli Regency Bali The villages residential area is surrounded by hilly areas ravines and fields belonging to the residents Within this village area there is a parent settlement complex that is the forerunner of the village The settlements are in four banjar areas namely in Munduk Lampah Banjar Tanah Daha Banjar Sukawana and Banjar Desa
