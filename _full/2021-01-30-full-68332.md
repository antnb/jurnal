  - "where the research process carried out is to use library materials from books or publications of law and legislation. The results of the study show that the legal regulation of a domain name against typosquatting in e-commerce activities is regulated in article 23 paragraph (1) of Law No. 19 of 2016 in amendments to Law No 11 of 2008 concerning Electronic Information and Transactions which determines a domain name must be based on law"
  - "propriety and good faith. Legal consequences that occur by typosquatting in e-commerce activities"
  - "namely typosquatting actions can be given domain name cancellation and administrative sanctions"
  - "which can be in the form of a written warning"
  - "revocation of domain names and revocation of operating status if after 14 (fourteen) days since the issuance of a written warning"
