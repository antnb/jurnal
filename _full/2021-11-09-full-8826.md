<h1><a name="bookmark0"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark1"></a>BY</span><br><br><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark2"></a>I KETUT EKA NUGRAHA</span></h1>

<p><span class="font1">Jurnal ini berjudul “The Comparison of The Compliments and Compliment Responses between </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">A Walk to Remember</span><span class="font1"> (American movie) and </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Love Actually</span><span class="font1"> (British Movie)”. Tujuan dari penelitian ini adalah untuk mengidentifikasi kategori pujian dan untuk membandingkan serta mengklasifikasikan jenis-jenis respon pujian yang digunakan film yang berasal dari Amerika dan Inggris. Data dalam Penelitian diambil dari dua film romantis </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">A Walk to Remember</span><span class="font1"> dan </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Love Actually</span><span class="font1">. Metode yang digunakan dalam menganalisis penelitian ini adalah metode deskriptif kualitatif. Teori pertama yang digunakan dalam penelitian ini adalah teori kategori pujian oleh Wolfson (1983) di dalam jurnal Qanbar. Teori kedua adalah teori merespon pujian oleh Herbert (1989) di dalam jurnal artikel Riyanto. Pembahasan data di awali dengan menonton film </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">A Walk to Remember</span><span class="font1"> dan </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Love Actually</span><span class="font1">, memberi tanda pada pujian dan respon pujian yang digunakan dalam film A </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Walk to Remember</span><span class="font1"> dan </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Love Actually</span><span class="font1"> pada percakapan mereka. Hasil dari pembahasan ini menunjukan bahwa ada perbedaan tanda pujian dan respon pujian yang digunakan dalam film yang berasal dari Amerika ( </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">A Walk to Remember</span><span class="font1">) dan film yang berasal dari Inggris ( </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Love Actually</span><span class="font1">). Dalam film-film Amerika lebih sering memberikan pujian dan merespon pujian karena dalam budaya Amerika , pujian adalah hal yang sangat wajar karena bisa memberikan kepuasan. Sedangkan dalam film Inggris lebih jarang kita dapat melihat seseorang memberi pujian dan pujian untuk menanggapi dalam budaya Inggris, Anda tidak akan dihargai untuk memuji orang pada hal-hal yang mereka tidak bisa membantu, dan pujian itu langka. Demikian pula , dalam budaya inggris memuji diri sendiri adalah hal yang tabu.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Kata kunci: comparison, compliments, compliment responses</span></p>

<h1><a name="bookmark5"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark6"></a>1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Background of the Study</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">One of the utterances that people often use in conversation through language is compliment and compliment response. Compliment is often found in daily conversation within people in the society. People give compliment to the others in order to establish a good social relationship. People give compliment in order to establish and maintain a good social relationship. Whenever compliment is uttered, there must be the reply as the response to the compliment. It is generally agreed that the polite thing to do is to accept compliment. In practice, however, compliments are not always accepted. In general, compliment is often used to create a good relationship in friendship, work, acquaintance and relationship or married couples. Basically, little or multitude of people is very happy to be praised because by compliment they feel very appreciated and cherished, particularly positive compliment, because generally has two different meanings, the first one is positive compliment, for example “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">you look so handsome</span><span class="font1">”, and the second one is negative compliment (insinuation), for example “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">hahaha (just laugh), nice jacket, but you didn’t fit</span><span class="font1">”.</span></p>

<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">5. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Analysis of the Comparison of the Compliments and Compliment Responses between </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">A Walk to Remember</span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"> (American movie) and </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">Love Actually</span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"> (British movie)</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;">
<li>
<h1><a name="bookmark13"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark14"></a>5.1 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Compliment Categories and Compliment Responses in </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">A Walk to Remember</span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"> (American Movie)</span></h1></li></ul></li></ul>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font1">1) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Conversation 1</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">Belinda: </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;text-decoration:underline;">Nice sweater</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Jamie: </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;text-decoration:underline;">Thank you</span><span class="font1">.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Analysis:</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font1">a) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Compliment: after Jamie arrived at school, Belinda praises Jamie by saying “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">nice sweater</span><span class="font1">”. Belinda praise Jamie because she looks beautiful using the sweater. Based on compliment, it was compliment on appearance.</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font1">b) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Compliment response: in the above conversation, it can be seen that there is a compliment response “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">thank you”</span><span class="font1"> found in Jamie’s utterance. It show Jamie accepted Belinda’s compliment on her personality by saying </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">“thank you”. </span><span class="font1">Jamie’s statement belongs to the </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Appreciation Token</span><span class="font1"> because she was saying </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">“thank you”</span><span class="font1"> although without nodded her head</span></p></li></ul>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font1">2) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Conversation 2</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">Teacher: Landon, </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;text-decoration:underline;">you were marvelous!</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Landon: </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;text-decoration:underline;">Thank you</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Analysis:</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font1">a) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Compliment: One of the </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Compliments on Skill or Ability</span><span class="font1"> can be seen in the conversation mentioned above. In this conversation, teacher looked praised Landon by saying “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">you were marvelous”</span><span class="font1">. This compliment called </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Compliment on Skill or Ability</span><span class="font1"> because Landon could plays his role very well.</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font1">b) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Compliment response</span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">: </span><span class="font1">from the above conversation, it can also be seen Landon responded teacher’s compliment by saying “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">thank you</span><span class="font1">”. This response was </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Appreciation Token</span><span class="font1">. This response called </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Appreciation Token</span><span class="font1">, because Landon accepted teacher’s compliment directly by saying “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">thank you</span><span class="font1">”, It showed compliment accepted by addressee.</span></p></li></ul>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark15"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark16"></a>5.2 Compliment Categories and Compliment Responses in </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">Love Actually</span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"> (British Movie)</span></h1></li></ul>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font1">1) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Conversation 1</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">Collin: </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;text-decoration:underline;">Beautiful muffin for a beautiful lady</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">The staff: </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;text-decoration:underline;">(no responses)</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Analysis:</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font1">a) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Compliment: in the morning at office, Collin gave the staff a muffin and he praised her by saying “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Beautiful muffin for a beautiful lady</span><span class="font1">”. It was compliment on personal trait. It was personal trait because Collin like with her personality.</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font1">b) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Compliment response: from the above conversation, although Collin praised her but she was silent (no response). That belongs to </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">No Acknowledgement. </span><span class="font1">This is called </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">No Acknowledgement</span><span class="font1"> because she did not respond Collin’s compliment, it seem with the addressee gives no indication of having heard the compliment.</span></p></li></ul>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font1">2) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Conversation 2</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">Juliet: </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;text-decoration:underline;">Oh, bingo. That's lovely. Well done, you.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;text-decoration:underline;">Oh, that's gorgeous</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Thank you so much, Mark, this is exactly what I was hoping for.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Mark: </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;text-decoration:underline;">(no responses)</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Analysis:</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font1">a) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Compliment: in this conversation, after Juliet arrived at mark’s house, Juliet asked her wedding video to mark and then she was watching the video. After that Julie praise mark by saying “Oh, bingo. That's lovely. Well done, you. Oh, that's gorgeous” it was compliment on skill or abilities because on the movie, Juliet praise mark because he could make a very nice video of her wedding with his skill and abilities.</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font1">b) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Compliment response: from the above conversation, although Juliet praised Mark but he was silent and he just smile (no response). That was belongs to </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">No Acknowledgement.</span><span class="font1"> This is called </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">No Acknowledgement</span><span class="font1"> because Mark did not respond Juliet compliment, it seem with the addressee gives no indication of having heard the compliment.</span></p></li></ul>

<p><span class="font1">Based on the analysis and discussions of the previous chapter, some conclusions can be drawn from this movie. This is the result of the comparison between compliment responses in </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">A Walk to Remember</span><span class="font1"> (American Movie) and </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Love Actually</span><span class="font1"> (British Movie). More American movie often give compliments and compliment responses. In America or in American culture, compliments and compliment responses are very reasonable thing because they can give satisfaction, because in America it is to say that communication is based on the goal of creating a sense of equality and belonging. This, in turn, is due to the fact that American culture rests on the belief in the primacy of the individual (not group) and that it is achievement-oriented, not oriented notion. Compared to movies from the British, British movies rarely someone gives a compliment and compliment response. In English culture, you will not be rewarded for praise people on things they could not help, and the rarer compliment. Similarly, in a culture that self-praise is taboo, praise be given more careful, because receiving a compliment is to praise oneself.</span></p>
