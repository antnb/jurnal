Gajah Oling is a special batik from Banyuwangi district which is still believed by the Using tribe in Banyuwangi as a distinctive and sacred motif used in various existing custom rituals and dances One of the traditional rituals that still uses the batik motif is Seblang dance ritual performed in two places Olehsari and Bakungan village In Olehsari village Seblang is performed by young girls meanwhile in Bakungan village Seblang is performed by elderly women
