<h1><a name="bookmark0"></a><span class="font1"><a name="bookmark1"></a>WRITING GUIDANCE</span></h1>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font0">1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Journal of Electrical, Electronics and Informatics is issued twice a year, each February and September.</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font0">2. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Technical paper is an original work none published yet or would be published to Otherjournal.</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font0">3. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Technical papers could be result from research formatted science or technology, research result, literature review study, methodology study, critical original idea, review of important issue in recent development.</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font0">4. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Technical paper is written in English. The organization of the paper includes Abstract, Introduction, Research Method, Results and Analysis, Conclusion, Thanks Giving (if any) and Reference. The paper length is maximum 8 pages including tables and figures.</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font0">5. &nbsp;&nbsp;Abstract should briefly summarize the essence of the paper and address objective,</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font0">technology or method, results, conclusions, and clinical impact.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font0">6. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Technical paper submitted for publication must advance the state of knowledge and must cite relevant prior work.</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font0">7. &nbsp;&nbsp;The paper is written according to the template of IRCS UNUD Journals.</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font0">8. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;All accepted papers should complete and sign the copyright form and publishing agreement form, and sent to </span><a href="mailto:jeei@unud.ac.id"><span class="font0">jeei@unud.ac.id</span></a><span class="font0">.</span></p></li>
