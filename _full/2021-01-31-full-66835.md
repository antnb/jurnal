ABSTRACT The purpose of this research is to analyze the arrangements in protecting the copyrighted work of audio podcasts under the copyright act and to know the legal sanctions in the event of piracy such as re-uploading audio podcasts work by other parties without the permission of podcasters. This writing uses normative legal research methods with a statutory approach and analysis of legal concepts. The results of the analysis showed that audio podcasts with a subject topic such as sermons"
  - "lecturers"
  - "addresses"
  - "or other works of utterance have been set out in Article 40 paragraph (1) letter b of the Copyright Act"
  - "while outside of these types of topics have not been regulated so that there is a blurring of norms regarding the absence of further explanation related to the phrase “other works of utterance”. For the act of unlawfully re-uploading audio podcasts by others for commercial purposes may be penalized as stipulated in the copyright Act by making a complaint of filing a claim for demages.
