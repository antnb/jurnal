The purpose of this study is to develop theoretical model and choose empirical model of the effect of income on consumption in Indonesia By using secondary data of two macro economics indicators ie Product Domestic Bruto and consumption estimation of chosen model is analyzed through Partial Adjustment Model and Correction Error Model The findings show that model with loglinear function is suitable for estimating the effect of consumption on income in Indonesia
