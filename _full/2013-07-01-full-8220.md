The research about utilization of natural dyes from root bark extract of Morinda citrifolia on cotton fabric has been done The extraction of root bark morinda was done by using water and methanol as solvent with ratio of 120 wv 10 g sample  200 mL solvent The extract obtained from water was blackish brown while from methanol solvent was reddish brown The extract obtained was used to dye cotton fabric with addition of 100 g whiting as a mordant Extracts
