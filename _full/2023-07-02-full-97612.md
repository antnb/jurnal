ABSTRACT This paper has the purpose of understand and analyze the Indonesian ius constitutum on criminal responsibility of perpetrator with disability in Indonesia and the imposition of criminal sanction for perpetrator with disability as an intellectual dader in a premeditated murder. This study used the method of normative with an laws and regulations approach. This study shows that the criminal responsibility of perpetrator with disability is regulated on Article 44 of the Criminal Code. The imposition of criminal sanction for perpetrator with disability as an intellectual dader in a premeditated murder in the form of criminal responsibility is still possible according to Article 340 of the Criminal Code and take the provision of Law Number 8 of 2016 as the consideration in imposing criminal sanction.
