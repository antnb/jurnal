<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">Abstrak</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Penelitian ini bertujuan untuk mengidentifikasi fungsi kesopanan dan menganalisa bagaimana makna kesopanan yang diterapkan pada percakapan dalam film Hotel Transylvania. Data yang dikumpulkan dari internet ditranskripsikan kedalam bentuk tertulis. Data dikategorikan berdasarkan teori yang dikemukakan oleh Brown dan Levinson, dan dianalisis secara deskriptif dengan metode kualitatif. Hasil dari penelitian ini menunjukan bahwa kesopnan diterapkan pada film dan berhubungan dengan beberapa fungsi dan makna. Fungsi </span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">assertive, directive, commissive</span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">, </span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">expressive </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">dan </span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">deklaratif </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">diterapkan oleh para karakter pada percakapan di dalam film. Makna kesopanan dipengaruhi oleh konteks situasi dan fungsi dari pernyataan tersebut. Makna tersebut disampaikan dengan strategi-strategi seperti </span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">bald on record, positive politeness, negative politeness </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">dan </span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">off record</span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Kata kunci: fungsi, makna, politeness</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark2"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark3"></a>1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Background of the study</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">Politeness is the application of good manners.However, what is considered polite in one culture can sometimes be quite rude or simply eccentric in another cultural context.The use of Politeness also serves several functions such as to avoid conflict, to ensure cooperative interaction, to manage impressions, to establish power, to ensure compliance, to show deference, and to be nice.These functions can be viewed as goals to be achieved and politeness is one of the relevant communication strategies.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>

<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What functions of politeness are used on the movie “Hotel Transylvania”?</span></p></li>
<li>

<p><span class="font2">The aims of this study are:</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>

<h1><a name="bookmark8"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark9"></a>4. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Research method</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The sources of this study are spoken and written data. The spoken data were taken from the movie “Hotel Transylvania”. The written data were from the transcription of the movie conversation.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">This study used documentation method and note-taking technique; the movie “Hotel Transylvania” was taken from website Youtube.com. The written data were collected by watching the movie “Hotel Transylvania” and it was transcribed into written form. The subtitles of the movie were used to support the validity of the transcription.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">The data were used to analyze the politeness sense in terms of intonation or how the politeness is expressed by the characters. The data were selected and qualitatively analyzed using the descriptive method. The data were categorized into four types of politeness based on the theory of politeness proposed by Brown and Levinson (1987) which was used to analyze the politeness meaning expressed by the characters on the movie and theory of illocutionary acts proposed by Searle in Leech (1983) which was usedto identify the functions of politeness.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark10"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark11"></a>5. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Result and discussion</span><br><br><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark12"></a>5.1 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Functions of politeness</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The theory of illocutionary acts proposed by Searle in Leech (1983:105) was used to analyze the functions of politeness expressed by the characters, such as assertive, directive, commissive, expressive dan declarative.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark13"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark14"></a>5.1.1 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Assertive</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">It is an act performed by a speakerto tell the truth on what he said. The point of the truth in this Assertive is neutral; it can be either true or wrong.The functions of this Assertive are</span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">stating, suggesting, reporting, complaining, claiming, and boasting</span><span class="font2">.There are two kinds of this function found in the conversation of the dialogue such as </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">stating and complaining.</span></p>

<h1><a name="bookmark15"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark16"></a>5.1.2 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Directive</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">It is an act performed by the S to make the H do what the S wants. The Directive speech acts include </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">asking for, inviting, insisting, suggesting, enjoying, charging, commanding, urging, pleading, challenging, gesturing, ordering, requesting, advising, and recommending</span><span class="font2">.Two functions of Directive were found in this dialogue of the movie; they are</span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">advising and commanding</span><span class="font2">.</span></p>

<p><span class="font2">It is the act that binds the S to carry out all the things mentioned in the utteranceand perform an action in the future, for example, </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">swear, promise, threaten, refuse, vow, and offering</span><span class="font2">.The function of commissive found in the dialogue was </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">refusing.</span></p>

Expressive speech act includes </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">saying thank you, congratulating, pardon, flattering, praising, blaming, and criticizing, appointing</span><span class="font2">.Two functions of the Expressive found in the dialogue of the movie were </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">thanking and praising</span><span class="font2">.</span></p>

<h1><a name="bookmark23"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark24"></a>5.2 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Meaning of politeness</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The data were analyzed using theory of the politeness strategies proposed by Brown and Levinson (1987:56). The types of politeness strategies found in this movie were bald on-record, negative politeness, positive politeness, and off-record (indirect).</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>

<p><span class="font2">This strategy is most often utilized in the situation where the S has a close relationship with the audience, such as relatives or close friends.Five sub strategies of bald on record found in this dialogue are the strategies of </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">maximum efficiency, metaphorical urgency for emphasis, metaphorical urgency for high valuation of H’s friendship, sympathetic advice of warnings, and permission that H has requested.</span></p>

<h1><a name="bookmark33"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark34"></a>6. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Conclusion</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The functions of Politeness found in the movie “Hotel Transylvania” are assertives in the forms of stating and complaining; directives in the forms of advising and commanding, expressive in forms of thanking and praising;commissive in the forms of refusing, and declarative in the form of naming.The meaning of politeness expressed in this movie is in the forms of four politeness strategies such as </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">bald onrecord, positive politeness, negative politeness</span><span class="font2">, and </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">off record</span><span class="font2">.The function of politeness was varied, same types or strategies of politeness may have different functions.The use of politeness strategies expressed by the characters of the movie has different functions based on the situation or the condition of both speaker and hearer.</span></p>
