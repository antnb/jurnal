Research has been done to identify the radioactive elements in the Citicon aerated lightweight concrete block sample In this research used a Citicon aerated lightweight concrete block as a sample with 1 cm x 3 cm in size and identification process used LIBS method Radioactive elements identified in Citicon aerated lightweight concrete block the LIBS method were Th Ra and K The number of photon emission intensity at the wavelength of each element is 396710 nm
