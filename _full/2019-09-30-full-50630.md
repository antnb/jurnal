This paper explores the renditions produced by the interpreters in sermon interpreting at ROCK Church Denpasar Bali It aims at finding out and investigating the categories and the motives of the renditions produced by the interpreters in the setting under investigated The method adopted for this study is a descriptive qualitative approach The data of this study is based on six transcribed audiovideo recordings of interpretermediated sermons on Sunday services
