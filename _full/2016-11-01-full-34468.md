Megalithic culture is culture which produces big buildings made of big stone The megalithic cultural relics are exist in Bedulu Village Blahbatuh District Gianyar Regency in form of stone mortar millstone menhir flat stone natural stone and sarkofagus This research was intended to know the function changes of the megalithic cultural relics or heritage in several temples in Bedulu Village such as Santrian Temple Dukuh Santrian Temple Samuan Tiga Temple
