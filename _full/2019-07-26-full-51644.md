The purpose of this research was to synthesize and characterize activated carbon from bamboo stem waste by using ZnCl2 solution as activator. The research was started by preparing the bamboo carbon through carbonization at a temperature of 650oC for 90 minutes. Activation was carried out by adding ZnCl2 with various concentration ratios. The results showed that the optimum concentration of ZnCl2 activator was 0.02 M. The activated carbon had a moisture content of 5.00%"
