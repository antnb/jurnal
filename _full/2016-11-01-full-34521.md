<p><span class="font2">Vieda Pra Ramadhani<sup>1*</sup>, I Nyoman Aryawibawa<sup>2</sup>, Sang Ayu Isnu Maharani<sup>3 123</sup>English Department Faculty Of Arts, Udayana University </span><span class="font0"><sup>1</sup></span><a href="mailto:viedaprar@yahoo.com"><span class="font0">[</span><span class="font2">viedaprar@yahoo.com]</span></a><span class="font2"> <sup>2</sup></span><a href="mailto:inyoman.aryawibawa@gmail.com"><span class="font0">[</span><span class="font2">inyoman.aryawibawa@gmail.com]</span></a><span class="font2"> <sup>3</sup></span><a href="mailto:isnu.maharani@yahoo.com"><span class="font0">[</span><span class="font2">isnu.maharani@yahoo.com]</span></a></p>

<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">Abstract</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Studi ini berjudul “The Use of Pictures as Stimuli in Children’s English Vocabulary Acquisition”. Studi ini dipilih untuk mencari keefektifan dari gambar sebagai metode pembelajaran dalam Bahasa Inggris khususnya dalam memperoleh kosa kata. Studi ini juga menjabarkan masalah-masalah yang dihadapi siswa dalam belajar Bahasa Inggris.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Data didapat dari siswa kelas dua sekolah dasar yang juga merupakan siswa Kumon. Mereka diberikan gambar dan diminta untuk menebak gambar dalam Bahasa Inggris. Untuk menemukan keefektifan gambar sebagai rangsangan, data yang didapat kemudian dianalisis dengan menggunakan teori mean score dari Stamboel (1982).</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Penggunaan gambar sangat efektif dalam proses perolehan kosa kata Bahasa Inggris, bisa dilihat dari prosentase jawaban benar dari siswa yang belajar menggunakan gambar lebih tinggi dari siswa yang belajar tanpa menggunakan gambar. Tidak hanya mencari keefektifan gambar, studi ini juga membahas masalah-masalah yang dihadapi siswa dalam belajar Bahasa Inggris. Faktor internal merupakan faktor yang berasal dari dalam diri siswa seperti; kemampuan menerima materi belajar, konsentrasi, rasa percaya diri, kemampuan mengingat kosa kata, kurangnya pemahaman Bahasa Inggris, dan kebanyakan siswa menganggap Bahasa Inggris adalah bahasa yang susah di pelajari. Sementa afaktor eksternal adalah faktor yang berasal dari lingkungan disekita rmereka seperti; kurangnya motivasi untuk belajar Bahasa Inggris, guru yang kurang berpengalaman dan media pembelajaran yang digunakan</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Kata kunci: </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">bahasa, gambar, keefektifan</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark2"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark3"></a>1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Background of Study</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">Language is used to communicate between two or more people to deliver meaning. People compete to learn English because indeed English is considered a second language. It is not surprisingly if there are some parents who want their children to become capable of speaking English by joining them in courses.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">Language acquisition refers to the process of natural assimilation. What children learn about language is determined by what they already know about the environment around them. It seems similar to the way of children in learning to </span><span class="font1">270</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">imitate their native speaker, a process of producing functional skill in the spoken language that is learned without theoretical knowledge.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark4"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark5"></a>2. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Problems of the Study</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">Based on background of study, there are some problems of research as follows: 1. How effective is the use of pictures as stimuli in teaching vocabulary?</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">2. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What the common problems are faced by children in English vocabulary acquisition?</span></p></li></ul>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark6"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark7"></a>3. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Aims of the Study</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The aims of this study are to know how pictures can be an effective stimuli for children in vocabulary learning especially in acquiring English vocabulary and to find out the common problems faced by children.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark8"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark9"></a>4. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Research Method</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The data in this research were based on experiments involving students in the second grade of elementary students at the age around 7 years old. They are from Denpasar. The method used in collecting data was the direct observation or teaching method that was done in the classroom during the English teaching-learning process, in order to collect the original data.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">The students were divided into two groups. One group was given practice for two times, while the other group was not given such a practice like the other group. The two groups were given a final test with the same material on the same day.</span></p>

<h1><a name="bookmark12"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark13"></a>6. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Conclusion</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">Based on the research and the analysis above, it can be concluded that indeed the use of picture was very effective in the process of acquiring English vocabulary, it can be seen from the percentage of the correct answer from the students whom were taught with pictures was higher than students whom were taught without pictures.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">Pictures were considered an effective method in teaching-learning process because children remembered words in English more easily; besides, children felt happy if learning English by using colorful pictures.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">Not only to find out the effectiveness of picture, this study was also to find out problems faced by the children in learning process. There were two factors that affected problems. The Internal factor which came from the inside of learners included the ability to receive the material such as concentration, self-confidence, and the ability to remember vocabulary; lack of understanding of English language, and the fact that the students mostly assumed that English wasdifficult to be learned. The external factors which came from the environment around students included the lack of motivation in learning English, the fact that teachers were lack of experiences, and the media used in teaching learning. However, the common problems faced were lack of concentration</span></p>
