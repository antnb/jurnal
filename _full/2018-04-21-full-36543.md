This study aims to compare the ability of CAPM and FF3FM in predicting stock return in Indonesia Stock Exchange Population of this study is companies listed on the Indonesia Stock Exchange which are included in the Kompas 100 Index in the period of 2012  2016 The results show that market risk premium variable has a positive effect to return on six portfolios formed in CAPM and FF3FM The variable size premium has a positive effect on SH SM and SL portfolio
