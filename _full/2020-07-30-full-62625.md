This paper deals with the constructions of passive and antipassive in Sasak KutoKute dialect SKD related to valency decrease The study involved 70 verbs proposed by Malchukov and Comrie 2010 and applying the theory of typology linguistics from Dixon 2012 for data analysis The finding showed that passive in this dialect can appear in three forms the use of morphological marking confix ke n syntactic marking preposition siq by and no markings at al
