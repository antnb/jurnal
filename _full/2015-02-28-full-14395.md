The aim of this study is to determine the mediating role of brand image on the influence of countryof origin on brand equity of Toyota car in Denpasar City This research was conducted to 150 samples by implementing purposive sampling method The data was collected by using instrument with 5 point Likerts Scale The data was analized with Structural Equation Modeling  SEM  technique The results show that 1 country of origin has a positive effect on brand image
