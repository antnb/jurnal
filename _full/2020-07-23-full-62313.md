Research that aims to determine the effect of commercial ration replacement with fermented green bean sprout waste flour on the physical carcass composition of male bali duck at the age of 8 weeks has been carried out at the Farm Faculty of Animal Husbandry Udayana University on Jl Raya Sesetan Markisa Gang No 5 Denpasar Bali The study used a completely randomized design CRD consisting of 3 settings and 5 replications Each repetition used 3 male Bali ducks
