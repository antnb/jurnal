ABSTRACT The necessity for writing this journal is aimed at analyzing and understanding legal protection for consumers who are disadvantaged and to find out the responsibilities of these online business actors. The research method used in this paper is normative research method. The existence of Law No. 8 of 1999 concerning consumer protection for consumers who are harmed by business actors for negligence made through online business accounts can obtain protection from business actor fraud. Business actors who commit default by using an account without clear information are subject to law No. 19 of 2016 concerning amendments to Law No. 11 of 2008 concerning Information and Electronic Transactions (UU ITE).
