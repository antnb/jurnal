A research on the variation of fingerprint patterns of Kailinese and Torajanese tribes have been done in Palu and Sigi Regency Central Sulawesi The aims of this research were to determine the variation of fingerprint patterns Dankmeijer Index Furuhata Index and Total Ridge Count of Torajanese and Kailinese The population was divided into three groups namely the Kaili Daa Kaili tribe which has phenotypic characteristics resembling the Australomelanesoid race
