<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">TABOO WORDS IN MOVIE “SUPERHERO MOVIE” AND “SCARY MOVIE</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">2”</span></p>

<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">Abstrak</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Judul tulisan ini adalah kata-kata tabu bahasa inggris yang digunakan dalam film “Superhero Movie” dan “Scary Movie 2”. Kata tabu adalah suatu adat budaya dalam berbicara dimana tidak diperbolehkan dalam situasi formal. Namun kini banyak film yang menggunakan kata tersebut karena kata tabu tersebut hanya mendukung alur cerita dan karakter tokoh film tersebut. Oleh sebab itu, kata-kata tabu menjadi topic utama dalam penelitian ini. Penelitian ini dilakukan untuk mengetahui fungsi kata tabu dalam percakapan yang ditemukan di film. Sehingga dari penelitian ini dapat diketahui adanya makna yang ingin disampaikan oleh pembicara terhadap pendengar. Data diambil dari film “Superhero Movie” dan“Scary Movie 2”. Data dikumpulkan dengan menggunakan metode observasi lalu dianalisis dengan penjelasan deskriptif.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Kata kunci: tabu, jenis, fungsi, Scary Movie 2, Superhero Movie</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li><a name="caption1"></a>
<h1><a name="bookmark0"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark1"></a>1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Background</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">Taboo words are something common in our daily conversation, although they are not allowed in formal conversation. In movies, taboo words are used to support the plot of the story. In this study, the use of taboo words is specially mentioned in informal condition or in a certain situation that may qualify in suitable manner and purpose.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark2"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark3"></a>2. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Problem of the Study</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">Based on the background above, in this investigation, the research problem is the function of taboo used in conversation in “Superhero Movie” and “Scary Movie 2”.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark4"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark5"></a>3. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Aims of the Study</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">To find out the functions of taboo words used in “Superhero Movie” and “Scary</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">Movie 2”.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark6"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark7"></a>4. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Research Method</span><br><br><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark8"></a>4.1 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Data Source</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The data of taboo words, which were analyzed in this study, were taken from the Hollywood movie </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Superhero Movie</span><span class="font2"> and </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Scary Movie 2</span><span class="font2">. Those films were chosen as data source because in the dialogues of the movies a lot of taboo/dirty words were used.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark9"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark10"></a>4.2 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Method and Technique of Collecting Data</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">In this writing, the method used is observation and the data were limited only to the conversations that contain taboo words. The observation was done through finding the English script on the Internet and watching the movies. Note-taking collect was done while watching the movies. All of the sentences that contain taboo words found in these movies were noted down to get the relevant data. After all the data were listed, then every taboo word was identified in order to find out the function of every taboo word found in the movies.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark11"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark12"></a>4.3 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Method and Technique of Analyzing Data</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The data were qualitatively analyzed by descriptive explanation based on the theory Functions of language proposed by Janet Holmes (1992).</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark13"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark14"></a>5. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Functions of Taboo Words in Movie </span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">Superhero Movie</span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"> and </span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">Scary Movie 2 </span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">5.1 Expressive Function</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The examples Expressive Function on Scary Movie 2 are as follow:</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Buddy </span><span class="font2">: Hey, Cindy, think fast! (Throw a ball over Cindy)</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Cindy &nbsp;&nbsp;</span><span class="font2">:Ohh! Uhh! Aah!</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Buddy &nbsp;&nbsp;</span><span class="font2">: Dude</span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">, </span><span class="font2">you </span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">suck</span><span class="font2">!</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">This scene is when Cindy is walking in house corridor. Suddenly Buddy throws a ball that hit Cindy’s head. The word “suck” shows that Buddy invoke Cindy. In this case, Buddy expresses his disappointed feeling about Cindy that she could not catch the ball. For this reason, this utterance is categorized under Expressive Function.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">The examples Expressive Function on Superhero Movie are as follow:</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">The Dragonflies pursue the Hourglass after he got the energy reactor. He tracks him until he arrives into a room that held a costume party. People there invoke the dragonfly as in this conversation:</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Dragonfly</span><span class="font2">: Hourglass !... hero con ?</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">People </span><span class="font2">: It's a convention for people who like to dress up as their favorite heroes and villains. By the way, your costume </span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">sucks</span><span class="font2">.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">The word </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">sucks</span><span class="font2"> was used to destroy the Dragonfly’s costume. For this reason, this utterance is categorized under Expressive Function.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark15"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark16"></a>5.2 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Directive Function</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The Directive Function on Scary Movie 2 shown as below:</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Cindy </span><span class="font2">: Yeah. It was traumatic. It really was...and it's taking me a while to get over it, but, you know...now that we're out of the house and back in school... I just know that everything's gonna be OK.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Bird </span><span class="font2">: Oh</span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">, God</span><span class="font2">, will you shut the fuck up?</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">In this conversation, Cindy is on the phone with her parent but the bird in her room complained and told Cindy to be quiet. For this reason, this utterance is categorized under Directive Function.</span></p>

<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark17"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark18"></a>5.3 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Referential Function</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The Referential Function on Scary Movie 2 is shown as follows:</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Buddy </span><span class="font2">: Guys, I think the Professor's up to something. I heard Dwight tell the Professor there could be a poltergeist in the house and that we could all be in danger!</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Shorty </span><span class="font2">: Oh, no, son! Not a poltergeist! What's a poltergeist?</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Theo </span><span class="font2">: I'm getting out of here.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Buddy </span><span class="font2">: No. We can't go anywhere. The gates are locked.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">Dwight's the only one who has the keys.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Ray </span><span class="font2">: Baby, get my Vaseline</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Brenda </span><span class="font2">: I'll handle</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Ray </span><span class="font2">: Get my Vaseline, a banana, and some duct tape.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Shorty </span><span class="font2">: I'm gonna break his legs.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Brenda </span><span class="font2">: I'm gonna </span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">fuck </span><span class="font2">that little cripple up!</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">This conversation takes places in the kitchen, while all gather up, buddy said that they are all in danger and the only one who have the keys of the gate is Dwight. Everyone wants to get the key. And also Brenda says “I'm gonna </span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">fuck </span><span class="font2">that little cripple up!” to give others information that she will get the key with a violent ways and also uses a word “fuck” that is just a emotional word. For this reason, this utterance is categorized under Referential Function.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">The Referential Function on Superhero Movie is as follows:</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Uncle </span><span class="font2">: How did you do that?</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Rick </span><span class="font2">: Uh, it's easier than it looks.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Uncle </span><span class="font2">: No, I don't think so. Why don't you tell me what's going on?</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Rick </span><span class="font2">: All right. Well, there is something, okay? But you have to keep it a secret. I think I have...superpowers.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Uncle </span><span class="font2">: I find that hard to believe.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Rick </span><span class="font2">: All right, all right, how about this? Go ahead and throw as many punches as you want. I bet you 100 </span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">bucks </span><span class="font2">you don't land one.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Uncle </span><span class="font2">: easy money</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">This scene of film is when Rick came into the kitchen while Trey and Uncle Albert are fixing some stuff. Rick tells that he has superpowers. But his uncle didn’t believe it. So Rick tells his uncle to punch him then he bet 100 bucks his uncle couldn’t land one. For this reason, the researcher categorizes this utterance under Referential Function.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark19"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark20"></a>5.4 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Phatic Function</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The Phatic Function on Scary Movie 2 shown as below:</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">A</span><span class="font2">: &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Oh, shit, Ray, you got a tattoo.</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">B</span><span class="font2">: &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Oh, shit. What's it say?</span></p></li></ul>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">A</span><span class="font2">: &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&quot;Ray!&quot; Oh, man.</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">B</span><span class="font2">: &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Oh, shit, you got a tattoo, too.</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">A</span><span class="font2">: Oh, really? Dude! </span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Dude</span><span class="font2">, what's it say?</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">B</span><span class="font2">: &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&quot;Fucked me. &quot;</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">This conversation is between Ray and his friend in his room. His friend said that Ray got a tattoo and so did his friend. The word “dude” refers to close friend and Ray says that because he wants a respond from his friend. For this reason, this utterance is categorized under Phatic Function.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">The Phatic Function on Superhero Movie shown as below:</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Boss</span><span class="font2">: Who the </span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">hell </span><span class="font2">are you?</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Rick</span><span class="font2">: I was wondering if you've got a job opening for...</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Boss</span><span class="font2">: Job?! How dare you come in here and ask me for a job?</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">This conversation shows that Rick asks about a job to be a reporter, but the temperamental boss asks a question with frustration feeling, In this case, the speakers need a response. The word used is “hell” to express the boss’s feeling. Actually, the man who acts like a boss is crazy. For this reason, the researcher categorizes this utterance under Phatic Function.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark21"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark22"></a>6. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Conclusions</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">From six functions, according to Holmes, there are only four functions related to the taboo words in Superhero Movie and Scary Movie 2. They are Expressive Function, Directive function, Referential function and Phatic function that can be found in the movie. It is considered that it is not related to the poetic function and metalinguistic function because poetic function focuses on a poem and is used in order to fulfill the need of aesthetic reason, meanwhile metalinguistic functions to comment on language itself that serve response of the language launched by the others. The function of taboo word itself depends on the movie scene or speaker’s feeling.</span></p>
