This study aims to obtain empirical evidence of earnings management after changes in corporate bond ratings The modified Jones model is used to identify companies that practice earnings management The population in this study is all bonds of nonfinancial companies that issue bonds and those that experience upgrades or downgrades of bond ratings in the period 20102016 The method of determining the sample in this study used purposive sampling to obtain 30 samples
