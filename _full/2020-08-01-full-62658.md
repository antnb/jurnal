<h1><a name="bookmark0"></a><span class="font3" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark1"></a>Kata Pengantar</span></h1>
<p><span class="font2">Puji syukur kami panjatkan kehadapan Tuhan Yang Maha Esa atas terbitnya Buletin Fisika Program Studi Fisika Fakultas Matematika dan Ilmu Pengetahuan Alam Universitas Udayana, Volume 21, No. 2, Agustus 2020.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">Dalam Buletin kali ini, kami memuat tujuh tulisan yang merupakan hasil penelitian dan studi </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">literature</span><span class="font2">, yaitu:</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">1.</span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;"> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Fingerprints Image Recognition by Using Perceptron Artificial Neural Network</span><span class="font2">, Muhammad Arif Budiman, I Gusti Agung Widagda</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font2">2.</span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;"> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Utilization of Gamma Co-60 Radiation for Chili Plant (Capsicum annuum L.) Breeding by Using a Physical Mutagen Method,</span><span class="font2"> Ni Made Purmita Sari, Gusti Ngurah Sutapa, A.A. Ngurah Gunawan</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font2">3.</span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;"> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Analysis of Radiation Dose of Patients on CT Scan Examination using Si-INTAN Application</span><span class="font2">, Elshaday S.B Siregar, Gusti Ngurah Sutapa, I Wayan Balik Sudarsana</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font2">4.</span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;"> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Effect of the Ceramic Burning Temperature on the Characteristics of the Emission Spectrum (LIBS) of Al and Transmittance (FTIR) of Al-O on Ceramic Body of BL-1 Type</span><span class="font2">, Nyoman Wendri, Aprilion Krisandi, I Gusti Putu Yoyok Irawan</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font2">5.</span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;"> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Analysis of Superconductor of Nd1Ba2Cu3O7 and Nd3Ba5Cu8O18 Phases Using FTIR Spectrophotometer</span><span class="font2">, I Gusti Agung Putra Adnyana, Made Sumadiyasa, Nyoman Wendri</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font2">6.</span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;"> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Identification of Free Radical in Main Stream Cigarette Smoke in Cigarette with Clove mix (Kretek) and Cigarette without Clove mix (White)</span><span class="font2">, Ni Kadek Nova Anggarani, D.J. Djoko H.S., Unggul P. Juswono</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">7. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Effects of High g Values on Growth and Chlorophyll Content in Hydrated and Dehydrated Wheat Seeds</span><span class="font2">, Sagar S. Jagtap and P. B. Vidyasagar</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">Kami mengucapkan terimakasih kepada semua pihak terkait yang telah membantu terlaksananya penerbitan Buletin ini, dan kepada pembaca kami mohon kritik dan saran yang membangun untuk kesempurnaan Buletin Fisika ini.</span></p>
