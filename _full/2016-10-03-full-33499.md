<h1><a name="bookmark2"></a><span class="font3" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark3"></a>Abstrak</span></h1>
<p><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Kata Tabu merupakan fenomena yang terdapat dalam bahasa, karena kata tabu merupakan yang vulgar dan dapat menyinggung orang lain dalam percakapan. Penelitian ini bertujuan untuk mengidentifikasi jenis, menjelaskanf ungsi dan menampilkan jenis kata-kata tabu yang mendominasi film Easy A dan The Change-Up. Jenis kata-kata tabu dapat diklasifikasikan dalam 10 jenis berdasarkan teori yang dikemukakan oleh Jay (1992) dan fungsi kata-kata tabu berdasarkan teori yang dikemukakan oleh Holmes (1992). Hasil dari penelitian ini menunjukan bahwa terdapat perbedaan dari kedua film, yaitu terdapat 10 jenis kata tabu, 5 fungsi kata tabu dan jenis Insult &amp;&nbsp;Slurs yang mendominasi film Easy A. Sedangkan dalam film The ChangeUp hanya terdapat 8 jenis kata tabu, 5 fungsi kata tabu dan jenis Vulgarity yang mendominasi film tersebut.</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">Kata kunci : sosiolinguistik, kata tabu, jenis, fungsi</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark4"></a><span class="font3" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark5"></a>1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Background of the Study</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font3">Taboo word is a forbidden word in a culture, because such a word is not allowed to be uttered, and it includes sex, death, genitalia, intermarriage and many more on the use of offensive language. Kusuma (2013) stated that taboo words are usually used in daily conversations. It can be found when people express their emotions, such as emotion of anger like “fuck”; “shit”, or shocked feeling like “holy shit”;”damn it”. Taboo words are a unique phenomenon, because they may produce embarrassment, disgust, and social norm violation in the society concerned.</span></p>

<h1><a name="bookmark10"></a><span class="font3" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark11"></a>4. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Research Method</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font3">The data were collected through observation method and documentation method. Observation is the first step method to collect the data before documentation method. Documentation method is the supporting method of this study. There were two steps in collecting the data. First, downloading the movies from </span><a href="http://www.ganool.com/easy-a-movies-2010.html"><span class="font3">http://www.ganool.com/easy-a-movies-2010.html</span></a><span class="font3"> and </span><a href="http://www.ganool.com/the-change-up-movies-2011.html"><span class="font3">http://www.ganool.com/the-change-up-movies-2011.html</span></a><span class="font3"> then watching every scene in detail and using English subtitle. Second, note taking the taboo words showed in the movies for every scene while watching the movies, the utterances which are considered appropriate data were noted. There are 20 scenes from </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Easy A</span><span class="font3"> and 25 scenes from </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">The Change-Up</span><span class="font3"> movie. But there are 10 scenes selected from </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Easy A</span><span class="font3"> and 12 scenes were selected from </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">The Change-Up.</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">A descriptive qualitative method was used to analyze the function and types of taboo words. After all the data collected, the data were classified based on the types according to Jay (1992) and the function of taboo words according to Holmes (1992). The data were analyzed and selected to a certain number needed for the type of data presentation with the purpose of describing the various types of taboo words and the</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">function of taboo words in the dialog. After the data were analyzed, the next step was counting the types of taboo words to see dominant types of taboo words in movies.</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">This study used the descriptive method in presenting the data related to the subject of this study. The data were categorized, depending on each scene. Furthermore, each scene was presented descriptively.</span></p>

<h1><a name="bookmark12"></a><span class="font3" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark13"></a>5. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Result and Discussion</span><br><br><span class="font3" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark14"></a>5.1 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Types and Function in Easy A movie</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font3" style="font-weight:bold;">Olive : </span><span class="font3">Call Micah. Tell him I said </span><span class="font3" style="font-weight:bold;">he’s an asshole</span><span class="font3">. Tell him that I confessed to giving him Chlamydia. (01:22:05)</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">Olive said </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">he’s an asshole</span><span class="font3"> to Mrs. Griffin in which “he” refers to Micah. This term is categorized belong to the insult type. Olive was mad because Micah lied to his mother that Olive who gave him Chlamydia. He covered his relationship with Mrs. Griffin by accusing Olive. This utterance is categorized as having the metalinguistic function.</span></p>

<h1><a name="bookmark15"></a><span class="font3" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark16"></a>5.1.1 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Dominant Type of Taboo Words Used in Easy A Movie</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font3">The total of taboo words that were analyzed based on the main character and supporting character’s conversation in </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Easy A</span><span class="font3"> movie are 18. The dominant type of taboo words in this movie are </span><span class="font3" style="font-weight:bold;">insult and slurs</span><span class="font3">. </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Easy A</span><span class="font3"> movie has complete types of taboo words, ten types of taboo words based on the theory developed by Jay (1992) are found in this movie.</span></p>

<h1><a name="bookmark19"></a><span class="font3" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark20"></a>5.2.1 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Dominant Type of Taboo Words Used in The Change-Up Movie</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font3">From the analyzed data, total of taboo words in </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">The Change-Up</span><span class="font3"> movie are 20 words. The taboo words that showed up in the movie had been categorized based on Jay theory. According to the third problem of this thesis, the dominant type of taboo words used in </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">The Change-Up</span><span class="font3"> movie is </span><span class="font3" style="font-weight:bold;">vulgarity </span><span class="font3">type.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark21"></a><span class="font3" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark22"></a>6. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Conclusion</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font3">This study compares Easy A and The Change-Up movies to see the difference between those movies. The differences are, Easy A movie has 10 types of taboo words and The Change-Up has 8 types of taboo words. The dominant type of taboo words in the first movie is Insult and Slurs while Vulgarity is the dominant type in the second movie. There were 5 functions of taboo words which had been identified in both movies; they are: expressive function, referential function, directive function, metalinguistic function, and phatic function.</span></p>
