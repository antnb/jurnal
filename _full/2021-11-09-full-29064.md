<h2><a name="bookmark4"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark5"></a>Abstrak</span></h2>
<p><span class="font2">Artikel ini berjudul &quot;</span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Derivasional Inggris akhiran dengan referensi The Jakarta Post</span><span class="font2">&quot;. Penelitian ini membahas proses pembentukan kata bahasa Inggris secara morfologi. Masalah dari Penelitian ini adalah bentuk, fungsi, dan makna dari derivasional akhiran yang ditemukan di </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">The Jakarta Post</span><span class="font2"> berita online. Penelitianini bertujuan untuk mengidentifikasi bentuk derivasional akhiran dan untuk menjelaskan fungsi dan makna dari derivasional akhiran yang ditemukan dalam sumber data.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">Sumber data dari penelitian ini diambil adalah versi online dari </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">The Jakarta Post </span><span class="font2">berita yang tersedia di</span><a href="http://www.thejakartapost.com/"><span class="font2"> www.thejakartapost.com.</span></a><span class="font2"> Metode dokumentasi digunakan dalam mengumpulkan data dalam penelitian ini. Data dari penelitian ini dianalisis dengan metode kualitatif. Teori yang digunakan dalam menganalisis data adalah teori morfologi yang diusulkan oleh Plag (2002).</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">Hasil analisis menunjukkan bahwa ada empat bentuk akhiran derivasional, yaitu nominal akhiran, verbal akhiran, akhiran adjectival, dan akhiran adverbial. Fungsi derivasional akhiran dapat dibagi menjadi dua, yaitu menjaga kelas akhiran dan mengubah kelas akhiran. Akhiran yang ditemukan dalam sumber data memiliki arti masing – masing.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Kata kunci: Morfologi, Akhiran, Derivasional</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h2><a name="bookmark6"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark7"></a>1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Background of the Study</span></h2></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">Morphology is defined as the branch of linguistics that deals with word forms, their internal structure, components of grammar that include word formation and how words are formed. Morphology can be divided into inflectional morphology and derivational morphology. Derivation is the morphological process in which a derivational affix is attached to a base form to create a new word. The topic of derivational suffix is chosen because it is frequently used in written text. It is hard to comprehend the English sentence correctly without understanding the process of word</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">formation especially the process of suffixation which can change the class and meaning of the words. </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">The Jakarta Post</span><span class="font2"> is very interesting to go in certain direction to find many English derivational suffixes. Thus, this study was conducted in order to identify the forms of derivational suffixes and explain the function and meaning of derivational suffixes.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h2><a name="bookmark8"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark9"></a>2. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Problems of the Study</span></h2></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">This study is concerned withthe following problems:</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What forms of derivational suffixes are used in </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">The Jakarta Post</span><span class="font2">?</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font2">2. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What are the functions and meanings of derivational suffixes presented in </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">The Jakarta Post</span><span class="font2">?</span></p></li></ul>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h2><a name="bookmark10"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark11"></a>3. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Aims of the Study</span></h2></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">Based on the problems formulated above, this study is aimed at:</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Identifying the forms of derivational suffixes used in </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">The Jakarta Post</span><span class="font2">.</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font2">2. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Explaining the functions and meanings of derivational suffixes used in </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">The Jakarta Post</span><span class="font2">.</span></p></li></ul>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h2><a name="bookmark12"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark13"></a>4. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Research Method</span></h2></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The data source of this study was taken from the online version of The Jakarta Post’s news that is available at www.thejakartapost.com. The data in this study were collected using the documentation method. The data of this study were analyzed using the qualitative method which means that the information gathered is not in numerical form. The data that were taken from the news were analyzed based on the form of derivational suffixes by applying the theory of morphology proposed by Plag (2002). Then, the analysis was continued by explaining the function and meaning of derivational suffixes using the same theory.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>

<h2><a name="bookmark42"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark43"></a>6. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Conclusion</span></h2></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">Based on the analysis, it can be concluded that there are four forms of derivational suffixes found in the data source</span><a href="http://www.thejakartapost.com/"><span class="font2"> www.thejakartapost.com.</span></a><span class="font2"> They are nominal suffix, verbal suffix, adjectival suffix, and adverbial suffix. </span><span class="font1">The f</span><span class="font2">unction of derivational suffix can be divided into two; they are class-maintaining suffix and classchanging suffix.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">Class-maintaining suffix can be sub-divided into two namely </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">noun from noun </span><span class="font2">and </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">adjective from adjective.</span><span class="font2"> Class-changing suffix can be sub-divided into </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">noun from adjective, noun from verb, verb from other word class, adjective from noun, adjective from verb</span><span class="font2"> and </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">adverb from other word class.</span><span class="font2"> Suffixes found in the data source have their own meaning, for example </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">the activity or result of, the status or position of, make (more) X, connected with, make a person, in a….manner, etc.</span></p>
