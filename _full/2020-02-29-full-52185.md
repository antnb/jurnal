Dedang songke or the activity of weaving traditional cloth songke is a tradition that is still widely practiced by housewives as the ancestral heritage of Manggarai Community especially in the Orchard of Ringkas The objectives of this study are 1 to find out the reasons why women in Ringkas Orchard made dedang songke as home based production 2 to find out the implications of dedang songke as home based production for the lives of the weavers their families
