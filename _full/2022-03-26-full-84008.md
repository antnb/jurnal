This study aims to analyze the presence of herding behavior on the SRIKEHATI Index for the period January 2 2020 to December 30 2021 Herding behavior can be identified through the relationship between stock return dispersion and market return In calculating stock return dispersion using the Cross Sectional Absolute Deviation or CSAD method This study uses data on the daily closing price of the SRIKEHATI index and data on the daily prices of 17 company stocks
