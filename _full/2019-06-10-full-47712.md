This study aims to determine the effect of the effectiveness of accounting information systems on employees performance with incentives and work motivation as moderators in Denpasar LPD The population of this study was employees at the 35 LPDs in Denpasar The sample was determined by using a purposive sampling method with a total sample of 99 people The methods of data collection were done by using questionnaire method and simple linear regression data analysis
