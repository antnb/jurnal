One of the other authorities concerned with having the concept of making electronic deeds is to certify transactions"

To overcome the conflict of norms that occurs"
  - "it is necessary to use the two articles as long as they have complied with the provisions of Article 16 paragraph (7) of Law Number 2 of 2014 and the fulfillment of the authenticity of the deed regulated in Article 1868 of the Civil Code.
