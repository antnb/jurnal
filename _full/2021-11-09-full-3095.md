The aims of the research were to find out the content of nutrition and to detect the borax and formalin on beef meat balls bakso sapi sold in Denpasar Samples taken from five brands of bakso sold in 8 supermarkets Analysis was undertaken for the content of ash fat water and protein and also a qualitative analysis to detect borax and formalin The results showed that many samples did not fulfill the standards SNI particularly on fat and water content None
