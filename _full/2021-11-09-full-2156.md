A total of 10 pigs were allocated to a treated group (T1) and control group (T0). Each group consisted of 5 animals. Animal in the treated groups received on injection 10 IU Oxytocin prior to artificial insemination. Animal in the control group received saline at 1 ml. The pigs were injected by intramuscularly in the neck. Pregnancy rate were monitored and litter size were determined for all animals. The data were analised by using t test.
