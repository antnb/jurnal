ABSTRACT The study examines the legal certainty of the regulation on the prohibition of concurrent positions carried out by DPR members and the legal aspects of DPR members who concurrently serve as commissioners of a limited partnership. The study of this type of normative legal research uses a statute approach and a conceptual approach. Based on the study results carried out on the legal issues raised"
  - "it found that the regulation on the prohibition of concurrent positions for DPR members is regulated through Article 236 of the MD3 Law. Then concerning the legal aspects of simultaneous jobs carried out by members of the DPR as commissioners of CV"
  - "it is not prohibited in positive Law. This because Article 236 paragraph (2) of the MD3 Law related to the prohibition of concurrent positions of DPR members as commissioners of limited partnership cannot be interpreted as a rule prohibiting DPR members from DPR members concurrently serving commissioners in limited partnership.
