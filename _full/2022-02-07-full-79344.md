The issuance of this regulation can also provide legal certainty in the arrangement and control aimed at street vendors. Street vendors according to Regional Regulation No. 2 of 2015 has rights and obligations. The right in question is the right to obtain business registration services for street vendors or TDU which can result in street vendors getting empowerment and assistance provided by the Denpasar City Government.
