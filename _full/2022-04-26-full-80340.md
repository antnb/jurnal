This study aims to analyze the effect of audit quality size of the board of directors and capital intensity on tax aggressiveness with gender diversity of directors as a moderating variable The research was conducted on mining companies based on the IDXIC classification on the IDX for 4 years 20162019 period The sample obtained was 47 observations with crosssectional data method and multiple linear regression test The results show that audit quality is negatively related to tax aggressiveness capital intensity is positively related to tax aggressiveness"
