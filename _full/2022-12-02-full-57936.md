This study aimed to analyze the procedures involved in translating English adverbials of manner into Indonesian The data were taken from a bilingual biography entitled Against All Odds A qualitative methodology was employed in this study Additionally the data were analyzed using the translation procedure theory proposed by Vinay and Dalbernet in Venuti 2010 The results of the data analysis are presented using a narrative approach and descriptive sentences
