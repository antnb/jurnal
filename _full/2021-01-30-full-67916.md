  - "Izin Edar ABSTRACT This scientific work aims to determine the legal arrangements for food home industry products without marketing authorization and know the responsibilities of businesses for food home industry products without marketing authorization. The approach in this research is the Legislative Approach or (Statue Appoach) and the Analytical and Conceptual Approach or Law (Analytical and Conceptual Approach). The method in this paper uses normative legal reserch methods. The results of this study explain Legal arrangements for food home industries without a distribution permit are enforced in accordance with the principle of lex specialis derogat legi generalis.
