The advantages of this cooling system was operated without using the electrical energy andenvironmentally friendly. This study uses electronic components Peltier for cooling. The changing of temperature in the cooler system was analyzed and obtained the performance of the cooling boxsystem. The experiments were carried out in open space which exposure to solar radiation.
