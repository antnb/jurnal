This study aims to determine the effect of the addition of various bovine serum albumin BSA concentrations on betsville thawing solution BTS extender to maintaining vitality and motility of spermatozoa on Landrace pigs This study used Completely Randomized Design CRD with four treatments namely T0 control were given BTS without BSA T1 were given BTS added BSA 5 mgml T2 were given BTS added BSA 10 mgml and T3 were given BTS added BSA 20 mgml
