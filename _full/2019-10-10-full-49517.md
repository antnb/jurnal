Audit quality is very important because high audit quality is expected to produce relevant and reliable financial statement information as the basis for decision making The purpose of this study was to determine the effect of care due professional independence and auditor work experience on audit quality with reward as moderating variables in KAP in Bali Province in 2018 The population of this study all auditors working in KAP in Bali amounting to 76 people
