This study was a descriptive correlational with cross-sectional design. Thesampling method used was non-probability sampling with purposive sampling technique. The samplesize of this study was 100 respondents which met the inclusion criteria. The instrument used in theprocess of taking and collecting data was Baecke Questionnaire and Spirometry Chestgraph Hi-101.Using non-parametric Rank Spearman statistical test on the data collected"
  - "it was found that the p valuewas 0.000.. (&lt;0.05) for each % predicted FVC and % predicted FEV1.0 which concluded that there wasa correlation between physical exercise routine with the lung function of young adult smokers.Correlation Coefficient found between Baecke sport index with % predicted FVC was r=0.714 andbetween Baecke sport index with % predicted FEV1.0 was r=0.726.
