Stem bark of Tenggulun Protium javanicum Burm has been used traditionally by Balinese people as antiinflammatory agents This research aimed to analyze phytochemical contents and to evaluate antiinflammatory activity of stem bark ether extract of Tenggulun Phytochemical study was done qualitatively using phytochemical reagents Antiinflammatory activity was evaluated on edema rats induced by carrageenan with given extract at doses of 125 250 and 500 mgkg
