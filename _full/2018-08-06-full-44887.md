<p><span class="font3" style="font-weight:bold;">Abstract </span><span class="font3">Retail business has a rapid development, especially in Indonesia. It is indicated by the increasing number of traditional retail business which begins to fix itself into a modern retail business or an independent modern retail business. The increased number of modern retail pushes the competition of the business world to the next level, so it requires retail entrepreneurs to optimize their strategy which known as </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">marketing mix</span><span class="font3"> retail. </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Impulse Buying Behavior</span><span class="font3"> as a result of the successful strategic marketing mix retail often wished to happen to indicate the existence of the retail business. Similarly, with the modern retail which exists in Denpasar city, who is trying to develop its marketing mix strategy in the hope of </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">impulse buying</span><span class="font3"> behavior. This research has a purpose of explaining the influence of </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Merchandise</span><span class="font3">, </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Atmospheric outlet</span><span class="font3"> and promotion toward </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Impulse Buying Behavior</span><span class="font3"> with positive emotion variable as an Intervening variable on modern retail in Denpasar city. To reach that purpose, 100 (one hundred) respondents are used which selected by </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">purposive sampling</span><span class="font3"> with a questionnaire as the method for data gathering. The data is processed with the SEM PLS analysis tool. The result shows that </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Merchandise</span><span class="font3"> variable, </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Atmospheric outlet</span><span class="font3">, and promotion has a positive influence significantly on Positive emotion. The same with </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Merchandise</span><span class="font3"> variable, </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Atmospheric outlet </span><span class="font3">and Promotion, and positive Emotion significantly influence the </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Impulse buying behavior.</span><span class="font3"> Positive Emotion variable significantly partially mediates the influence of </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Merchandise</span><span class="font3">, </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Atmospheric outlet</span><span class="font3">, and Promotion about </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Impulse buying behavior</span><span class="font3">.</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">Keywords: </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Impulse Buying Behavior</span><span class="font3">, Modern Ritel</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font3">I. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;INTRODUCTION</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Impulse Buying Behavior</span><span class="font3"> is a phenomenon and an expanding tendency of shopping behavior that happens in the market and happens to be an important point that was underpinning the marketing activity [1]. </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Impulse Buying</span><span class="font3"> is also happening to be one of the unique characters that Indonesia’s civilian has. According to the winner of </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">the top brand award</span><span class="font3"> in 2009, Indonesia consumers has ten unique characters, which are short-term thinking, unplanned, like to assemble, technology illiterate, context-oriented, prefer abroad’s brand, religious, prestigious, strong in the subculture, and less concerned about the environment [2].</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">One of the impulsive shopping factors is emotion from the consumers while shopping. Preference [3] shows positive emotion that consumers feel will push them to acquire a product immediately without any planning at first and on the other hand, negative emotion can push consumers to not do impulsive shopping.</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">Reference [4] says that the retail marketing mix consists of location, </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Merchandise</span><span class="font3">, </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">pricing</span><span class="font3">, advertisement and promotion, the atmosphere in an outlet, and retail service. The retail company must ensure the position of its company is according to the customer’s expectation. Some important aspect that must be measured to know the power of retail, for example, the store location, store layout, diversity and product management, pricing strategy, a promotion that able to attract consumers, a service that</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">provided to strengthen and launch an identical marketing strategy.</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">Reference [5] says that </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Atmospheric outlet</span><span class="font3"> positively and significantly able to push them to do unplanned shopping. </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">The atmospheric outlet</span><span class="font3"> from a place or a restaurant is an important factor that will affect all of the experience that obtained from that current place [6].</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">Unplanned buying (</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Impulse Buying</span><span class="font3">) is a kind of purchase that was not planned by the consumer before, where this situation is purposely created by the retail entrepreneur, as their effort to survive in the industry. Some modern retail in Denpasar cities like Tiara Dewata, Matahari Duta Plaza, Ramayana, and other modern retail always optimize the occurrence of </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Impulse Buying</span><span class="font3"> with </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Merchandise</span><span class="font3">, Atmospheric outlet arrangement, promotion, and other strategies.</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">Based on the background, the issues in this research are how the influence of Merchandise variable, Atmospheric outlet, Promotion against Positive emotion and Impulse buying behavior, as well as how the roles of positive emotion to mediate the influence of variables for </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Merchandise</span><span class="font3"> management</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">, Atmospheric outlet,</span><span class="font3"> and Promotion about </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Impulse buying behavior.^</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">The purpose of this research is to know how is the influence of </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Merchandise</span><span class="font3"> variable</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">, Atmospheric outlet, </span><span class="font3">Promotion against Positive Emotion and </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Impulse buying behavior</span><span class="font3">. Another purpose is to know the role of Positive emotion in mediate the influence of Merchandise variable</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">,</span></p>
<p><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Atmospheric outlet,</span><span class="font3"> and promotions against </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Impulse buying behavior</span><span class="font3">.</span></p>

<p><span class="font3">II. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;THEORETICAL BASIS</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;">
<li>
<p><span class="font3">2.1 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Impulse Buying</span></p></li></ul></li></ul>
<p><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Impulse Buying</span><span class="font3"> is an unplanned buying, is a behavior of buying that conducted in the store, where the buyer is different from what has been planned before[7]. The unplanned purchase is an act of purchasing without any planning before, buying decision is done while the consumer is inside the store. Impulsive buying happens when consumers suddenly feel a strong feeling to buy immediately [14].</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font3">2.2 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Merchandise</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font3">Retailers activity include trading that sells product and direct service to the consumers. </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Merchandise</span><span class="font3"> is the products that retail sell toward the consumer in store, meanwhile merchandising can be interpreted as an effort of procurement and handling of goods [5]. Merchandising is an activity of goods’s procurement that compatible with the type of business that the store has (food type product, clothing, household needs, general product, etcetera, or combination) to serve in store with a compatible quantity, time, and price in order to reach the goal and store or retail company. Whereas according to American Marketing Asociation (quoted from Davidson ,1988) define Merchandising as “</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">The ways in providing the right Merchandise, at the right price level, in the right amount, in the right place and in the right time</span><span class="font3">” this definition later on known as </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">The 5 Right of merchandising</span><span class="font3"> [5].</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font3">2.3 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Atmospheric outlet</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font3">Store design is one of the important strategies in retail business. The atmosphere that has been designed will make customers feel comfortable to be in the store. The outlet’s looks will define the outlet into the customer’s mind. Outlet's design that will produce comfort and attractiveness for every customer's visit. Reference [8] shows atmosphere within an outlet is: </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Atmospheric refers to the design of an environment via visual communications, lighting, colors, music, and scent to stimulate customers perceptual and emotional responses and ultimately to affect their purchase Behavior.</span><span class="font3"> Store Atmosphere that “</span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Atmosphere refers to the store’s physical characteristics that are used to develop an image and draw customers</span><span class="font3">”[5]. The most fundamental thing in store design is “basically; store design aims to fulfill functional requirements while serving a fun shopping experience, so it supports the transaction</span><span class="font3" style="font-weight:bold;">.&quot;</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font3">2.4 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Promotion</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font3">Retail business related to the marketing of goods (or services) that needed by the community, either it’s individual or households. It is necessary for every business ventures and executes the marketing process is to inform the existence of our business to the community and</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">potential costumers. Discussion about the crowd means talking about</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">Their minds and emotion. Reference [9] says modern marketing needs more than to develop a good product, determine an interesting price, and make them reachable. The company must also communicate with interested parties now and the future, and the general community. The promotion will be an activity to inform our business to the community. Reference [5] says promotion or one-way persuasion is made to lead someone or organization to an act that creates a trade in the market.</span></p>

<p><span class="font3">Someone can position certain emotions, such as happiness, anger, joy, and sadness. For example, if someone feels very unhappy and compelled, then that person is not feeling happy. On the other hand, if someone feels very happy and pretty passive, then that person probably is happy. Psychologicthe al approach put its view towards human's behavior that human's behavior is influenced by the environment.</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">Reference [10] show &quot;Emotion is reaction assessment (positive or negative) of a complex nervous system of a person towards external or internal stimuli and often conceptualized as a general dimension, such as the positive and negative influences.&quot; It means that Emotion is an assessment reaction (positive or negative) from a human's neural system toward external or internal stimulus and often conceptualized as a general dimension, such as things that have positive or negative influence.</span></p>

<p><span class="font3">3.2 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Research Methodology</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font3">Based from the characteristic of the problem, the type of this research is associative research which aims to explain the influence of </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Merchandise</span><span class="font3"> variable, </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Atmospheric outlet</span><span class="font3"> and promotion against </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Impulse Buying </span><span class="font3">with positive emotion as the </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Intervening</span><span class="font3"> variable.</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">This research is done in some retails in Denpasar city, considering Denpasar city as the central government and business which make the mobility of its citizen higher, for example when using modern retail as a place to fulfill needs. It can be used as a barometer for strategy’s reference of the same industry in different places.</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">The population of the research is all citizens who have been purchasing needs spontaneously(unplanned) in modern retail in Denpasar city. The quantity of sample that defined for this research is 95 respondents with the consideration that the size of the sample is five times from the number of indicators that available (5 x 19 = 95 respondents). Sampling technique in this research is </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">a nonprobability sampling</span><span class="font3"> technique, which is a sampling technique that doesn't give the same opportunities or chance for every element or the member of the population to be chosen as sample. Sampling technique in question is a </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Purposive Sampling</span><span class="font3">, which is a sampling technique with certain criteria. The sample criteria are the sample has been shopping in one of the retails in Denpasar, and has done </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">Impulse</span><span class="font3"> Buying.</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">Variable measurement in this research uses a Likert scale. This scale is used to measure attitude, opinion, and response somebody toward an activity. In this closed-question questionnaire use the Likert scale between 1 to 5, where 1 represents a measurement of strong disagreement, 2 represent a disagreement, 3 represent a neutral statement, 4 represent an agreement, 5 represent a strong agreement.</span></p>
<p><span class="font3">Data collection technique is done in 2 ways, by questionnaire, which is a question list that addressed to respondents related to research's indicators.</span></p>

<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font3">a. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Rating </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">outer</span><span class="font3"> model or </span><span class="font3" style="font-style:italic;">measurement</span><span class="font3"> model</span></p></li></ul>
