This study aims to determine the Implementation of Work Perfomance Community TB Workers in Badung Regency 2019. The design of this study was cross-sectional observational descriptive. The sample in this study was that all tuberculosis cadres in Badung Regency gathered 50 people. Data obtained by interview techniques using questionnaires.
