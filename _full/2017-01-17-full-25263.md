The study aims to analyze the influence of the audit fee and professionalism of auditors on audit quality and job satisfaction as mediation at firms of public accounting in Bali with auditors on seven offices KAP as many as 81 people as a sample by sample method saturated The data collection is done by distributing questionnaires and analysis techniques using path analysis The analysis finds audit fee and auditor professionalism positive effect on audit quality
