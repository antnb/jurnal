  - "Hukum Pidana ABSTRACT The aim of this research is to analyze the legal aspects of supervising donation collection actions held by philanthropic institutions in the perspective of Indonesian criminal law. The results of the study show that foundations as philanthropic institutions are regulated for supervision for the scope of internal with foundation supervisors having the authority to supervise the activities of foundations. While external supervision is carried out when the foundation has acted as a collector of money and goods (donations) under direct supervision by the Ministry of Social Affairs. In addition"

The regulation of criminal sanctions for misuse of donated funds is contained in Article 70 in conjunction with Article 5 of Law Number 28 of 2004 concerning Amendments to Law Number 16 of 2001.
