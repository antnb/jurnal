This experiment to determine the productivity of Panicum maximum with different types and doses of bio slurry The experiment was conducted in a greenhouse for 3 months The design was complete Randomized Design RAL factorial pattern  The factor were firstly the types of bio slurry these types consisted of cow dung bio slurry BS and pig manure bio slurry BB The second factors was bio slurry fertilizer dosage  which were as a control without any fertelize
