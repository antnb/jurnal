This research aims to know the influence of family psychoeducation against family stress levels treating people with mental disorders (ODGJ) at home in the region Puskesmas II Denpasar Timur. This research is pre-experimental study (One Group Pre Post Test Design). The sample consisted of 30 people selected by purposive sampling technique. In looking at the family stress levels before and after the family psychoeducation used the questionnaire Zarit Burden Interview (ZBI). Research results was testing with paired t-test"
