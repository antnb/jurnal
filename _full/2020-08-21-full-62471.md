This study uses a normative legal research method with a statutory approach and analysis of legal concepts as well as a descriptive qualitative analysis method. This study shows that the provision of additional criminal payment of replacement money in corruption cases in Indonesia was originally regulated in Article 16 paragraph (3) Perpu Number 24 of 1960 concerning Investigation"
