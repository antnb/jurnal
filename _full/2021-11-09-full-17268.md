<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">THE SEMANTIC ANALYSIS OF TECHNICAL TERMS USED IN SURFING</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">by</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">Ni Putu Yolanda Indiani</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">English Department, Faculty of Letters and Culture, Udayana University</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Abstrak</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Studi ini membahas teknikal terminologi yang digunakan dalan surfing. Tujuan dari studi ini adalah mengidentifikasi jenis makna dari teknikal terminology yang digunakan dalam surfing yang terdapat di skrip film “Soul Surfer”. Metode yang digunakan untk memilih data pada studi ini adalah metode obervasi dan teknik pengambilan catatan. Studi ini menganalisis secara kualitatif berdasarkan teori makna dari Ogden dan Richards (1923) dan teori jenis-jenis makna dari John Lyons (1996).</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Hasil analisis dalam studi ini menunjukkan bahwa dari teknikal terminology dalam surfing yang terdapat dalam skrip film “Soul Surfer”dapat digolongkan ke dalam jenis referential (denotational) meaning, ideational (mentalistic) meaning, meaning-in-use meaning, ,dan behaviorist meaning. Namun tidak satupun dari teknikal terminology tersebut yangtermasuk ke dalam verificationist meaning dan truth-conditional meaning, karena teknikal terminology yang terdapat dalam skrip film “Soul Surfer” dalam bentuk kata dan frasa bukan dalam bentuk kalimat.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">Keyword : Teknikal Terminologi, Makna, Surfing.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Background of the Study</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">English language is rich in vocabulary including the ways of expressing meaning. One word could have more than one meanings. Hornby (2010:920) stated that, meaning is the thing or idea that a sound, word, sign, etc. represents. A word could have a different meaning in different field and context.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">Technical terms can be found in almost every field in our life with their own meaning and purposes. According to Webster (2000:531), </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">‘technical’</span><span class="font2"> means, “having special knowledge especially of a mechanical or scientific subject, or relating to technique”, meanwhile, </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">‘term’</span><span class="font2"> means “a word or expression that has a precise meaning in some uses or is limited to a particular subject or field”, Webster</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">(2000:534). So it can be conceptualized that </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">‘technical term’</span><span class="font2"> is “a word or expression which is used in particular subject or field”.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">In surfing, there are many terms found which are used as the name for every trick that is done by the surfer. Some of the terms have the same meaning as the words which are used in daily life. However, some of the terms have a totally different meaning from the common words. Based on this phenomenon, the technical terms used in Surfing which become the universal terms analyzed in this study. From the technical terms used in surfing, this study tried to figure out the meaning of the terms itself and explain the terms clearly.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">2. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Problems of the study</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">Based on the background mentioned above, there are two problems that are formulated in this study:</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">a) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What surfing technical terms are found in the </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">“Soul Surfer”</span><span class="font2"> movie script?</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font2">b) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What types of meaning can be identified in the technical terms used in surfing?</span></p></li></ul>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">3. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Aims of the study</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">Based on the problems of the study, the aims of the study are:</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">a) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To find out the technical terms used in surfing found in the </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">“Soul Surfer”</span><span class="font2"> movie script.</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font2">b) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To classify the types of meaning that can be identified in technical terms used in surfing.</span></p></li></ul>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>

<li>
<p><span class="font2">b) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Types of Meaning of the Technical Terms Used in Surfing</span></p></li></ul>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">- &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Data 1: Alana is paddling into a big wave on the </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;text-decoration:underline;">outside</span><span class="font2">.</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2" style="text-decoration:underline;">‘Outside’</span><span class="font2"> means the area outside of the lineup or break line where surfers in the lineup initially observe sets of waves as they approach. Often a term used to warn other surfers in the lineup that a new set of waves is approaching. </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">&quot;</span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;text-decoration:underline;">Outside</span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">!&quot;</span><span class="font2"> is the same as &quot;out-the-back&quot; (often used by Australian surfers.) According to Hornby </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">outside</span><span class="font2"> is a noun which means “the outer side or surface of something” (2010:1044). For example, </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">“The </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;text-decoration:underline;">outside</span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;"> of the house needs painting.”</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">Refering to the semantic triangle, the word ‘outside’ cannot be directly connected to ‘the area outside of the lineup or break line where surfers in the lineup initially observe sets of waves as they approach’ nor to ‘the outer side or surface of something’. The word should be defined based on the concept that is associated in the mind of the speaker and the hearer. If the participants have a concept of surfing in their mind, the word ‘outside’ can be defined as the area outside the line up of the surfers. However, if the participants do not have a concept of surfing associated in their mind, it can only be defined as the outer part of something or other things </span><span class="font1">4</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">depending on the experience of the participants. The context of situation is also an important part in order to define the meaning of a word. The term ‘outside’ here is used in the context of surfing, so it can be defined as the area outside the line up.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">From the definitions of the term above it can be seen that the meaning of theh term used in surfing has a different meaning from the term listed in the dictionary. However the meaning of the term used in surfing refers to the meaning of the term listed in the dictionary in terms of the position which both term means ‘in the outer side of something’. So it can be concluded that this term belongs to the </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">referential (denotational) meaning.</span><span class="font2"> Moreover, because this term is used to warn the other surfers this term can also be categorized into the </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">behaviorist meaning</span><span class="font2"> because it evokes a response from the other surfers.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">- &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Data 2: Big snap </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;text-decoration:underline;">off the top</span><span class="font2">!</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2" style="text-decoration:underline;">‘Off the top’</span><span class="font2"> is a technique where a surfer make a turn on the top of a wave, either sharp or carving. For example, </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">“Gets a nice vertical </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;text-decoration:underline;">off the top</span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;"> there as she kicks out to the challengers.”</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">The context of situation is an important part in defining the meaning of a word. The term ‘off the top here is used in the context of surfing, so it can be defined as a surfing technique. Referring to the semantic triangle, the term ‘off the top’ should be defined by the concept that is associated in the mind of the speaker and the hearer. It cannot be directly defined as a technique where a surfer make a turn on the top of a wave, either sharp or carving. It can only defined as a surfing technique if the concept of surfing is associated in the mind of the participants. If the participants do not have the concept of surfing in their mind, the term ‘off the top’ can only be defined based on the experience of the participants.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">This phrase cannot be found in the dictionary because it is not commonly used in daily life. However, this term can be understood by the people who surf. That is why it can be concluded that this term belongs to the </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">ideational (mentalistic) meaning.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">- Data 3: It looks like the local </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;text-decoration:underline;">groms</span><span class="font2"> have tracked down Hawaiian surfing</span></p>
