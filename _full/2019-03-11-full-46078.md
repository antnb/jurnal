The purpose of this study is to test and analyze the variables that affect the level of underpricing in companies that conduct IPOs These variables are the percentage of shares offered company size return on equity current ratio and debt to equity ratio This study uses a multiple linear regression analysis method The type of data used is secondary data The population of this study was 121 companies that conducted IPOs on the Indonesia Stock Exchange in 2013
