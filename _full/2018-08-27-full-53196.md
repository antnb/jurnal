The support required from family can be facilitated by forming support groups supervise the DM Type 2. This research aims to determine the effect of family as support group towards the decreases in blood sugar levels of the elderly with DM type 2. The design of this research is quasy experimental which used pretest-posttest with control group that was done on 20 different sample chosen in systematic sampling divided into two; 10 persons in control group and 10 persons in treatment group. The data analysis for before and after intervention uses Wilcoxon test showed the family as a support group effect on decrease blood sugar levels the elderly with DM type 2 which is obtained p value: 0.005 ? 0.05; and the difference test of blood sugar level changes uses Mann-Whitney test which gives the value of p=0"
