This study is focused to the speech of apology found in Japanese novel entitled Houkago ni Shisha wa Modoru by Akiyoshi Rikako and Balinese novel entitled Tresnane Lebur Ajur Satonden Kembang by Djelantik Santha The aims of this study are to know the functions and factors that affect and also the comparison of apology speech between Japanese and Balinese languages Theori use in this study is theory of level politeness factors proposed by Mizutani and Mizutani 19
