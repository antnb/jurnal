The method used is normative legal research method with statutory approach and conceptual approach. The results showed that opioid substances contained in kratom plants are also present in heroin and fentanyl narcotics"
  - "where heroin and fentanyl belong to class I narcotics. Kratom plants should be classified into class I narcotics due to the content of opioid substances contained in them. However"
