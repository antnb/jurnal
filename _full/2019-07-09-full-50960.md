  - "corruption develops because the weaknesses of system reinforcement or lack of public interests formation in practice and political policy. Therefore"
  - "strengthening bureaucratic ethics internalization is the most effective way to overcome corruption from its roots. Such political ethics must be established and developed together with the ongoing democratization"
