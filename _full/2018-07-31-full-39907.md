Adat law has been narrowly understood mainly as part of private law in the curricula of Indonesian law schools This is in contrary to the original intent of adat law both as an academic and policy discourse at the first place which was as an attempt to develop a legal system that is suitable to govern the Netherlands East Indies NEI for preventing violation against the indigenous sense of justice This article seeks to clarify the actual purpose of Adat Law
