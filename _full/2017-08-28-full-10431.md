This research aimed to find out correlation of coping strategy and quality of life in patients with gynecological cancer undergoing chemotherapy. This is a non experimental research (Descriptif Correlation). The research carried out in Cempaka Timur Ward at Sanglah Hospital in Denpasar. Questionnaires about coping strategy and quality of life were given to 62 respondents included in inclusion criterias. The results of the 62 respondents indicate that the majority of respondents coping strategies are in very good category and respondents quality of life are in good category. Based on the results of a statistical analysis by Spearman Rank with 95% level of significancy"
