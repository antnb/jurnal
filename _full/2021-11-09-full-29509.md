<h2><a name="bookmark2"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark3"></a>Abstrak</span></h2>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Studi ini berjudul “Semiotic Analysis of Four Popular ʻMemesʼ in memecenter.com”. Studi ini menggunakan empat macam meme yaitu, “Oh God” meme, “Forever Alone” meme, “Poker face” meme dan “Fuu” meme. Adapun studi ini bertujuan untuk mencari tahu penanda dan petanda dari keempat meme yang digunakan, serta mencari tahu fungsi dan deskripsi dari masing-masing meme sesuai dengan rumusan masalah yang diterapkan.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Teori yang digunakan untuk menganalisis penanda dan petanda dalam masing-masing meme adalah teori tentang semiotic oleh Ferdinand de Saussure (1983), untuk menganalisis fungsi dari meme itu sendiri menggunakan teori oleh Pierce (1885), dan untuk menganalisis deskripsi dari meme sesuai konteks situasi adalah teori yang dikemukakan oleh Dell Hymes tentang S-P-E-A-K-I-N-G model. Data pada studi ini didapatkan dari internet dalam situs memecenter.com yang menyuguhkan segala bentuk meme. Data dipilih dengan cara diamati, lalu mengklasifikasikan meme sesuai dengan meme yang digunakan dalam studi ini, kemudian data yang sudah diklasifikasiakan didownload, data pada studi ini dianalisis dengan metode kuantitatif. Data yang didapatkan ditampikan dalam bentuk deskripsi.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Setiap meme yang digunakan dalam studi ini memiliki penanda dan petanda. Masing-masing meme memiliki fungsi khususnya tersendiri karena meme pada dasarnya diciptakan dari ekspresi wajah manusia dalam bentuk kartun. Ketika meme digunakan dalam sebuah postingan di memecenter.com, di dalamnya pasti memiliki tema dan bagaimana meme itu mendeskripsikan situasi dan kondisi di dalam postingan.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Kata kunci: meme, fungsi, penanda dan petanda, deskripsi</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h2><a name="bookmark4"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark5"></a>1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Background of the Study</span></h2></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">Internet is very famous nowadays for satisfying people with various services related to various different fields. It is a very versatile facility which can help you complete many tasks easily and conveniently with few clicks. It can be any work of daily usage or any specific service which needs a lot of research and formalities to be done beforehand. Nowadays, people have made use of internet for various purposes in order to meet their needs. People use internet to earn a living, support their jobs, access social networks, or just to entertain themselves after exhausting daily activities. Starting</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">from a need for entertainment from the internet, sites that contain jokes began to emerge. Among the sites thatgive you some of entertaining things, there is one site which is more popular among the other sites that people usually visit,that is, memecenter.com. The jokes that were posted on memecenter.com come from various backgrounds, but all the people who visited the site understand the </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">memes</span><span class="font2"> and used it, the typical comical sign used in memecenter.com.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h2><a name="bookmark6"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark7"></a>2. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Problems of the Study</span></h2></li></ul>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What are the signifiers and signified of the </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">memes</span><span class="font2"> used in memecenter.com?</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font2">2. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What are the function and the description of the </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">memes</span><span class="font2"> used in the post?</span></p></li></ul>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h2><a name="bookmark8"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark9"></a>3. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Aims of the Study</span></h2></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The aims of the sudy are to find out the signifier and signified of the memes, and also to find out the function and descripions of the memes when they are used in the post.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h2><a name="bookmark10"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark11"></a>4. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Research method</span></h2></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">This study was conducted through three steps, determination of data source, data collection and how the data were analyzed.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">4.1 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Data source</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The data to be analyzed in this study were taken from the memecenter.com. This study took four popular </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">memes.</span><span class="font2"> Actually, there are many kinds of </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">memes</span><span class="font2"> based on specific regions, for example, the original </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">memes</span><span class="font2"> of Indonesia, Java </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">memes,</span><span class="font2"> Western </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">memes</span><span class="font2"> etc. This study took western </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">memes</span><span class="font2"> to be analyzed, they are “Oh God” meme, “Poker face” meme, “Forever Alone” meme and “Fuu” meme.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">4.2 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Method and Technique of Collecting Data</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">Library and online research. First, in order to get some information about the theory of semiotics, context of situation, meaning, the concepts of signs, library research were applied, it is needed to be used in analyzing the second problem of this study. Next, online research was applied to collect the data from memecenter.com.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">There were three steps in collecting data from internet, The steps were observing, classifying, and downloading the meme. The </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">memes</span><span class="font2"> were observed on the website of the </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">memes</span><span class="font2">, it is memecenter.com. Then, the writer classified the </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">memes</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">based on the four </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">memes</span><span class="font2"> used in this study, they are; “Oh God” meme, “Poker Face” meme, “Forever Alone” meme and the last is “Fuu” meme.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">4.3 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Method and Techniques of Analyzing Data</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The data analyzed based on the theories applied in this study. There are three theories used; they are the theory of semiotics by Saussure, theory of signs by Pierce, and the S-P-E-A-K-I-N-G model from Dell Hymes. First, to find out the signifiers and signified of the </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">memes</span><span class="font2"> using the Saussure’s theory. Second to find out the function of the memes and how the memes describe the current situation when they are used in the post based on theory proposed by Pierce and Dell Hymes.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>

<h2><a name="bookmark17"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark18"></a>5.2 . The Function and Descriptions of the </span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">Memes</span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"> Used in M</span><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">emecenter.com</span></h2></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">Every </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">meme</span><span class="font2"> hastheir own specific function that appears in the post. Firstly the writer classified the signs in the post, which one is symbol, icon, or index. Secondly, the writer finds out the theme of the meme, every </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">meme</span><span class="font2"> used in the post in memecenter.com must have a theme. The last is the function and description of the meme that can be assumed by seeing how the meme and the other sign act and what they are representing that time based on the theme applied in the post.</span></p><img src="https://jurnal.harianregional.com/media/29509-2.jpg" alt="" style="width:316pt;height:125pt;">
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">There is a symbol that can be seen in the post, there are;</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">a. </span><span class="font2">A “heart” on the letter representing or symbolizing a love.</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Icon/iconic</span><span class="font2">: a mode in which the signifier is perceived as </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">resembling</span><span class="font2"> or imitating the signified (recognizably looking, sounding, feeling, tasting or smelling like it) - being similar in possessing some of its qualities: e.g. a portrait, a cartoon.</span></p>
<h2><a name="bookmark19"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark20"></a>There are some icons that appear in this post, they are;</span></h2>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">a.</span><span class="font2"> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The letter</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">b.</span><span class="font2"> &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The mailbox</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">Index/indexical</span><span class="font2">: a mode in which the signifier is </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">not arbitrary</span><span class="font2"> but is </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">directly connected</span><span class="font2"> in some way (physically or causally) to the signified - this link can be observed or inferred.</span></p>
<h2><a name="bookmark21"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark22"></a>There is an index that appears in this post, there are;</span></h2>
<p><span class="font2">There is a mail in mail box means someone has sent it; in this case, the character got a mail and the one who send it is himself because he is so sad and lonely that no one ever pay attention to him.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">The theme of the story is about loneliness. This is about the loneliness felt by the character on the story because no one cares about him.According to the symbol, icons, index and the theme of the meme above, it can concluded that the function of “Forever alone” </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">meme</span><span class="font2"> is used to represent a facial expression of someone who is sad, because he lives without any companion and he is pretending to be okay.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">The situation in the post is described by “Forever alone” meme. The series of events are analysed as follows:</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">a. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Setting and Scene</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The setting in this post is in front of the main character’s house. Scene is the “psychological setting” of a scene. The psychological setting in this post is the sad feeling of the character.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">b. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Participants</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The participants are the speaker and the hearer in this story. In this case, the participant in this story is the main character himself.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">c. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;End</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The end of this story is the sad feeling of the character, no one put an eye or even care about him. The end of this story is also emphasized by the “Forever alone” meme by indicating that he is in the state of sadness and pretending to be okay.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">d. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Act Sequence</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">Act sequence is the form and order of the event. The story of the post begins when the character looks in the mailbox and gets a letter, but the truth is the letter is sent by him to himself.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">e. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Key</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The key in this post is the “Forever alone” meme and the expression that shows us a facial expression of someone who is “sad”, it means that the main character undergoing a hard life because no one cares about him.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">f. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Instrumentalities</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">Instrumentalities are the form and style of speech. This post is using some kind of daily spoken English.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">g. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Norms</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">It must be hard for people to live a life by their own, we need someone or companion because human cannot live their life by themselves.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font2">h. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Genre</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The story is telling a one-shot experience of the main character in a comic story. This story is more or less just like an anecdote, it is a short interesting story of the main character in the story.</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">From above analysis, it can be concluded that the “Forever alone” meme describes and emphasizes the psychological setting in this post, the character’s feeling shows that he is so desperate, lonely and sad because no one puts an eye on him or even cares a bit about him. The end of the story is also emphasized by the “Forever alone” meme, indicates of someone who is sad</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h2><a name="bookmark23"></a><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark24"></a>6. Conclusion</span></h2></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The four </span><span class="font2" style="font-style:italic;">memes</span><span class="font2"> used in this study are “Oh God” meme, “Poker face” meme, “Forever alone” meme, and the last is “Fuu” meme which has signifier different from each other, so that the signified would be different from each other and there is no meme which is having the same signified.Different memes have different functions, and they could also describe the current situation when they are used in the post.</span></p>
