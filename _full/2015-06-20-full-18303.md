This study aims were to determine the effect of tofu waste flour and wheat flour on the characteristic of flakes and to find out the best ratio of tofu waste flour and wheat flour that produce flakes with the best characteristic The design of research was Completely Random Design CRD The treatment factor was ratio of tofu waste flour and wheat flour with 5 levels  2080 2575 3070 3565 and 4060 Each treatment was repeated three times in order
