This study aims to determine the total number of bacteria isolated from laying hens at various ages The sample used was healthy laying hens feces with 24 samples using the pouring method This study uses a completely randomized design CRD The data obtained were tested with the Least Significant Difference Test LSD The results showed the total number of bacteria in the starter phase of layer was 5283x107 CFUml grower phase of layer was 20850x107 CFUml
