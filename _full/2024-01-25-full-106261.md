  - "to find out the legal consequences of sanctions for violations of piracy of electrctronic copyrighted works (E-Book) without the permission of the copyright owner. This study method is normative legal research using a conceptual approach legislation approach. The results showed that e-book literary works are protected literary works regulated in Law No. 28 of 2014 on Copyright where daIam Article 40 Paragraph (1) letter n implicitly mebenaraakan that e-books or digital reading is one of those in the work of adaptation that can be protection and as for the legal consequences of sanctions for violations of piracy of literary works of novels in the form of e-books without the permission of the creator where the perpetrators of piracy can be requested for compensation by filing a lawsuit to the commercial court in accordance with Article 99 of Law No. 28 of 2014 on Copyright.
