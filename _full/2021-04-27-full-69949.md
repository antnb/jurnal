ABSTRACT The purpose of this study is to examine a legal certainty in the regulation of billboard tax law in Denpasar City and specifically examine the problem of norms in the form of vague norms in the provisions of Article 3 paragraph (3) of the Denpasar Regulation No. 2 of 2011 related to exempt tax objects. The research method in this study is normative legal research with a type of statutory approach. The results of this study explain that the legal problems examined by the author are related to the regulation of advertisement tax law in the city of Denpasar which is regulated through the Perda Kota Denpasar No. 2 of 2011 and Perwali Denpasar No. 3 of 2014. Then related to the implementation of cloth billboards in the form of banners by the intra-campus Ormawa which is based on the Tri Dharma of Higher Education and is not intended for commercial purposes"
