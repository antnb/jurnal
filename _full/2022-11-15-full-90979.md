This research was conducted to see that effect of using golden snail as alternatif fed for Muscovy ducks on different ration level to hematologi profil and blood fat 64 Muscovy ducks of 8 weeks of age were use in this study use a randomized block design with four treatments and four replications whereas each replicate consisted of four male Muscovy ducks The composition of the ration used consisted of corn rice bran pollard coconut meal and golden snail meal
