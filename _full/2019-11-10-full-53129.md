<h1><a name="bookmark0"></a><span class="font4" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark1"></a>Determination of The Oxide Mineral Contents of the Tegal Lenga Beach Iron Sand by Using XRD</span></h1>
<p><span class="font2">P. Suardana<sup>1*</sup>, I B. Sujana. M<sup>2</sup>, Nyoman Wendri<sup>3</sup></span></p>

<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">Abstract – </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">The research of determination of the oxide mineral contents of the Tegal Lenga Beach iron sand by using XRD has been done. Iron sand samples were taken from the top layer of the Tegal Lenga Beach sand. The three sand samples were taken from three different places which were separated at a distance of 15 m one another. The separation process is carried out using magnetic separation method. The characterization methods include mass fraction and X-ray diffraction (XRD). It obtained that the average mass fraction of the Tegal Lenga Beach iron sand is 54.71%. The minerals oxide contained are magnesium diiron (III) oxide (Mg Fe</span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">2 </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">O</span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">4 </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">and iron iron (III) titanium aluminum magnesium vanadium manganese silicon oxide (Fe</span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">4.42 </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Fe</span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">5.245 </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Ti</span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">4.72 </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Al</span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">0.7 </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Mg</span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">0.4 </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Cr</span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">0.3 </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">V</span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">0.15</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">) (Fe</span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">7.82 </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Mn</span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">0.114</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Si</span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">0.06</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">) O</span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">32</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">). The main oxide mineral compound is Magnesioferrite (MgFe</span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">2</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">O</span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">4</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">) oxide mineral of 51.34%.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">Key words: </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Mineral oxide, Tegal Lenga Beach iron sand, X-ray diffraction (XRD)</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">Abstract – </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Penelitian penentuan kandungan mineral oksida pasir besi Pantai Tegal Lenga dengan menggunakan XRD telah dilakukan. Sampel pasir besi diambil dari lapisan atas pasir Pantai Tegal Lenga. Tiga sampel pasir diambil dari tiga tempat berbeda yang dipisahkan pada jarak 15 m satu sama lain. Proses pemisahan dilakukan dengan menggunakan metode pemisahan magnetik. Metode karakterisasi meliputi fraksi massa dan difraksi sinar-X (XRD). Diperoleh bahwa fraksi massa rata-rata pasir besi Pantai Tegal Lenga adalah 54,71%. Mineral oksida yang terkandung adalah magnesium diiron (III) oksida (Mg Fe2 O4) dan besi besi (III) titanium aluminium magnesium vanadium silikon oksida mangan (Fe4.42 Fe5.245 Ti4.72 Al0.7 Mg0.4 Cr0.3 V0.15) (Fe7.82 Mn0.114Si0.06) O32). Senyawa mineral oksida utama adalah mineral oksida Magnesioferrite (MgFe2O4) yaitu 51.34%.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">Kata kunci: </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Mineral oxide, pasir besi Pantai Tegal Lenga, X-ray diffraction (XRD)</span></p>
<p><span class="font2">iron sand and the dried-cleaned iron sand are weighed. Finally, the dried-cleaned iron sand were characterized by XRD.</span></p>

<p><span class="font2" style="font-weight:bold;">4. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Conclusion</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font2">The determination of the oxide mineral contents of the Tegal Lenga beach iron sand by using XRD has been done. It obtained that the average mass fraction of the Tegal Lenga Beach iron sand is 54.71%. The minerals oxide contained are magnesium diiron (III) oxide (Mg Fe</span><span class="font0">2 </span><span class="font2">O</span><span class="font0">4</span><span class="font2">) and iron iron (III) titanium aluminum magnesium vanadium manganese silicon oxide (Fe</span><span class="font0">4.42 </span><span class="font2">Fe</span><span class="font0">5.245 </span><span class="font2">Ti</span><span class="font0">4.72 </span><span class="font2">Al</span><span class="font0">0.7 </span><span class="font2">Mg</span><span class="font0">0.4 </span><span class="font2">Cr</span><span class="font0">0.3 </span><span class="font2">V</span><span class="font0">0.15</span><span class="font2">) (Fe</span><span class="font0">7.82 </span><span class="font2">Mn</span><span class="font0">0.114</span><span class="font2">Si</span><span class="font0">0.06</span><span class="font2">) O</span><span class="font0">32</span><span class="font2">) with the volume fraction of each compound are 62.95%, 39.78% and 39.74%. The main oxide mineral compound is Magnesioferrite (MgFe</span><span class="font0">2</span><span class="font2">O</span><span class="font0">4</span><span class="font2">) oxide mineral of 51.34%.</span></p>
