<p><span class="font0" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">ABSTRAK</span></p>
<p><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">Google.com merupakan situs terkenal yang menyediakan banyak informasi yang dapat diakses dengan media internet. Setiap iklan layanan masyarakat (ILM) di google.com mengandung tanda-tanda verbal dan non-verbal yang memiliki pesan dan fungsi. Penelitian ini menggunakan beberapa iklan layanan masyarakat dari google.com yang menyampaikan beberapa pesan yang membuat orang lebih sadar tentang kesehatan dan keselamatan di jalan raya. Pesan yang disampaikan dalam bentuk pernyataan yang menginformasikan pembaca yang biasanya dinyatakan dengan bentuk kata-kata dan gambar untuk membuatnya lebih menarik. Sebagian besar dari mereka menggunakan makna konotatif yang membuat orang lebih tertarik untuk tahu lebih banyak tentang pesan itu.</span></p>
<p><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">Kata kunci: iklan layanan masyarakat, google, pesan</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li><a name="caption1"></a>
<h1><a name="bookmark0"></a><span class="font0" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark1"></a>1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Background of the Study</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font0">There are many media which help us get new information in our daily life.</span></p>
<p><span class="font0">The information can be accessed through magazines, newspapers, books, and also from the internet. Internet is one of the easiest media to be found nowadays, because the internet can be accessed through our mobile phone or other gadgets. There is a site that is called </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">google.com</span><span class="font0"> which is mostly use web browser in the World Wide Web, because almost all information can be accessed on that site. </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">Google</span><span class="font0"> Inc. provides website </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">google.com</span><span class="font0"> as the main product and provides </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">Google+</span><span class="font0">, </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">Gmail</span><span class="font0">, </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">Google</span><span class="font0"> Translate, </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">Google</span><span class="font0"> News, </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">Google</span><span class="font0"> Chrome, and many other features.</span></p>
<p><span class="font0">In </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">google.com</span><span class="font0"> itself there are many public service announcements (PSA) that raise the awareness of the reader to make them more care about their life and also their environment. Public service announcement (PSA) which is usually called public service advertisement is totally different from the objective of a 1</span></p>
<p><span class="font0">standard advertisement that is to market a product. It is intended to change the public interest by raising awareness of an issue, affecting public attitudes, and potentially stimulating action.</span></p>
<p><span class="font0">The most topics of PSAs are about health and safety while driving on the road because nowadays the people are careless with their health and safety while driving on the road. The health and safety topics of the PSA are chosen because both of them are the most critical part of the day. A typical PSA is part of a public awareness campaign to inform or educate the public about an issue such as obesity or safety way in doing something.</span></p>

<h1><a name="bookmark8"></a><span class="font0" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark9"></a>4.1 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Data Source</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font0">The data for this study were obtained from </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">google.com</span><span class="font0"> because in </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">google.com</span><span class="font0"> there are many examples of public service announcement (PSA) provided. Therefore it makes this study easier in finding the best examples that can give the best result.</span></p>
<p><span class="font0">From </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">google.com</span><span class="font0"> there are two examples taken as data source of this study. They are: Public Service Announcement (PSA) by Denver Water Colorado about water consumption and Public Service Announcement (PSA) by Colorado State Patrol about tailgating on the road.</span></p>
<p><span class="font0">They were chosen as the data sources because they were focused on health and safety while driving on the road which is coherent with the topic of this study.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>

<p><span class="font0">The data were collected using documentation method. The techniques which were used to collect the data consist of several steps. The first step was that the sources were searched for the search engine of </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">google.com</span><span class="font0">. And then the best public service announcements (PSA) were chosen in order to have the best example. Finally, the publisher of each PSA was identified, as well as the verbal and non-verbal signs of the PSA.</span></p>

<h1><a name="bookmark26"></a><span class="font0" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark27"></a>6. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Conclusion</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font0">From the previous discussion, there are some conclusions which can be drawn as follow:</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font0">1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;This study identified two Public Service Announcement (PSA) on the </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">google.com</span><span class="font0"> which convey some messages that intend to make the people more aware of their health and safety on the road. The message itself is represented in verbal signs which is in the form of statement or texts and non-verbal signs in the form of picture or using some attractive colors.</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font0">2. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Most of the PSA in this study have informational function which are intended to remind the people of something as well as to remind the people of doing something safely and soundly. For example, safety on the road is like “do not tailgating” by Colorado States Patrol and about health which tells the safety way in using something like “use only what you need” by Denver Water.</span></p></li></ul>
