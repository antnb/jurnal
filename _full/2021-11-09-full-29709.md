<h2><a name="bookmark4"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark5"></a>Abstrak</span></h2>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Skripsi ini berjudul Analisis Psikologi pada tokoh utama di dalam drama Bang Bang You’re Dead” oleh William Mastrosimone. Tujuan penelitian ini untuk menganalisis aspek psikologi dan metode karakterisasi dari tokoh utama. Data dianalisis menggunakan metoda deskriptif kwalitatif berdasarkan teori Bernhardt (1953) dan William Kenney (1966). Sumber data penelitian ini diambil dari naskah drama “Bang Bang You’re Dead”</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Di dalam penelitian ini, ada dua permasalahan yang di bahas yaitu psikologi dari tokoh utama dan metode karakterisasinya. Terdapat tiga aspek dari psikologi tokoh utama yaitu perilaku, perasaan dan emosi, dan dorongan manusia.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Kata kunci: tokoh utama, aspek psikologi, metode karakterisasi</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h2><a name="bookmark6"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark7"></a>1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Background of the study</span></h2></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">Literature is the individual expression of human being which emphasizes on feeling, thought ideas, spirit, faith and also contains many value of life. Literary work can be divided into two elements such as non-fiction and fiction literary works.In drama as the fiction literary work, there are two elements needed to build a story, they are intrinsic and extrinsic elements. Character as one aspect of intrinsic element has an important role in building up the story, which is very interesting to analyze because it can guide us to the author’s thought and feeling expressed through their words.Mostly the stories as made by the writers are based on the writer life or those mostly as imitation of human being. That is why as an element of the story, character is very important and very interesting to analyze. Though it is only a story usually the characteristics and the acts of the characters in the story are presented as those of real human beings. This study was analyzed more deeply about the main character in the drama “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Bang Bang you’re Dead”</span><span class="font1">by William Mastrosimone. The reason to analyze the</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">main character is because the main character takes an important part in the story and appears continually form beginning until the end of the story.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>

<p><span class="font1">The problem based on what has been described in the background section. In this research, can be formulated as follows:</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font1">1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What are the psychological aspects of the main character in the drama </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">“Bang Bang You’re Dead”?</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font1">2. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What kinds of methods of characterization are used to describe the main character in the drama </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">“Bang Bang You’re Dead”?</span></p></li></ul>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>

<p><span class="font1">In writing this paper, there are two specific aims to be achieved.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font1">1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To find out the psychological aspects of the main character in the drama </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">“Bang Bang You’re Dead”.</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font1">2. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To find out the methods of characterization are used to describe the main character in the drama </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">“Bang Bang You’re Dead”.</span></p></li></ul>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h2><a name="bookmark12"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark13"></a>4. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Research method</span></h2></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">The data in this study were taken from the drama script </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">“Bang Bang You’re Dead”</span><span class="font1">. The collecting data was done by watching the drama to understand the script of drama, identifying and classifying the dialogues, and then note taking the dialogues that are related to the topic. The data was analyzed by using descriptive technique. There are two steps in analyzing the data. The first step started from classifying the collected data into certain categories. Second step is analyzing the data using the theory of psychology proposed by Bernhardt (1953) and methods of characterization proposed by William Kenney (1966). This study would be done with the conclusion of the analysis and become the answer of the problem being mention above.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
