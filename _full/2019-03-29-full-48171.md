This study aims to determine the effect of drying temperature on the bioactive component contents of white peony tea and to know the proper drying temperature in white peony tea to produce the highest bioactive component contents and the best sensory characteristics The experimental design used was Completely Randomized Design with a drying temperature as the treatment consisting of five levels 60oC 70oC 80oC 90oC and 100oC The treatment was repeated 3 times
