Underpricing is a phenomenon that often occurs from IPO activities on the IDX Underpricing is the difference in stock prices that occur in the primary market and secondary market where the bid price is lower than the closing price of the first trading day The purpose of this research is to find out the effect of accounting information and investor demand on underpricingThis research was conductedat companies whose IPO on the Stock Exchange in 20162018
