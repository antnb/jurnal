  - "it aims to comprehend how regulations pertaining to the protection of Human Rights can be implemented at sea"
  - "specifically in the high seas"
  - "which are essentially unclaimed territories not belonging to any state. Secondly"
  - "it seeks to explore the obligations of states in the high seas to safeguard the Human Rights of individuals aboard ships. The author will utilize a normative legal research methodology to address these concerns. Consequently"
  - "it can be inferred that international laws concerning the protection of Human Rights can be applied by invoking Article 293 of UNCLOS"
  - "which allows for the incorporation of relevant international laws. Furthermore"
  - "state obligations in the high seas emanate from the flag state's exclusive jurisdiction over the ship. Hence"
  - "the flag state bears the responsibility to ensure that the ship adheres to all applicable national and international laws"
  - "in accordance with the flag state's jurisdiction. Key Words: UNCLOS"
