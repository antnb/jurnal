This research is classified as a normative legal research that uses a statutory approach and a comparative approach. The results of the study show that the regulation of green open space in urban areas based on regulations in Indonesia is regulated in Undang- Undang Nomor 26 Tahun 2007 Tentang Penataan Ruang and Peraturan Menteri Dalam Negeri Nomor 1 Tahun 2007. However"
