The paper presents the results of research aimed at the acoustic and perceptual analysis of onset and coda clusters from standard Slovak that violate the principle of sonority The research is based on Engstrand and Ericsdotters 1999 claim that clusters violating sonority are perceptually advantageous over those that follow the sonority principle The analysis was carried out on a sample of test nonsense words with the initial combinations sp st sk and final
