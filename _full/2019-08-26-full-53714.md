  - "there are many ways to support chronic illness patients. One of them is telenursing. Telenursing as a process of giving"
  - "managing and coordinating of care and administering health services through information technology and telecommunications. This research aims to know the influence of Telenursing to the self care patient ability. The intervention given for three weeks. This was quasi experimental with pretest-posttest using control group design. The sample technique was a purposive sampling. The number of sample were 60 people consisting of 30 people in control group and 30 people in treatment group. The Mann-Whitney test obtained p value = 0.000 &lt; 0.05 which means there is significant influence of telenursing self-care ability both on treatment group and the control group. So it can be concluded that there is significant influence of the Telenursing to the self care ability. Keywords: telenursing"
