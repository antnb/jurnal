<p><span class="font0" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">Abstrak</span></p>
<p><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">Bahasa kemahiran para guru Bahasa Inggris Sekolah Menengah Kejuruan di Bali merupakan tolak ukur kemahiran dalam berbahasa Inggris dalam proses belajar mengajar kepada para siswanya. Kemahiran Bahasa Inggris para guru SMK dilakukan lewat ujian nasional. Ujian Nasional TOEIC 2012 yang dilaksanakan pada tanggal 5 Mei 2012 oleh kedutaan Inggris di Jakarta sebagai Panitia Penyelenggara. TOEIC merupakan Tes Bahasa Inggris Internasional untuk komunikasi menjadi ukuran dalam pengajaran Bahasa Inggris di Sekolah Menengah Kejuruan di dalam penunjang kurikulum nasional pengajaran Bahasa Inggris di sekolah-sekolah di Indonesia</span><span class="font0">. </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">TOEIC 2012 diikuti oleh 700 peserta termasuk 75 guru Bahasa Inggris Sekolah Menengah Kejuruan yang merupakan perwakilan dari 8 kabupaten dan 1 Kotamadya di Bali. Hanya 75 guru yang berasal dari Bali mendapatkan hasil TOEIC 2012 dibawah Standart Nasional. Hasil dari TOEIC 2012 ini di analisa dan dikaji dengan menggunakan metode kuantitatif dan kualitatif.</span></p>
<p><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">Ada dua faktor yang mempengaruhi hasil TOEIC 2012 masih dibawah standard nasional. Faktor-faktor itu adalah : faktor psykologi dan faktor non psikologi. Toeri yang digunakan teory hyme and George Hillock yang dihubungkan oleh hasil TOEIC Listening, Reading, Writing dan Speaking</span><span class="font0">.</span></p>
<p><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">Keywords: Tujuan TOEIC, 2 faktor dan Standard Nasional</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li><a name="caption1"></a>
<h1><a name="bookmark0"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark1"></a>1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Background</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">English language proficiency in the English teacher of State Vocational High School should be good as grammartical and accordance with the curriculum set by the central government The Minestry of Education and Culture Republic of Indonesia. This achieving expected target in teaching learning maximally with the best graduated able to master the English language of Teaching Communication Curriculum.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">The reality based on TOEIC 2012 examination followed by 75 English Teacher of State Vocational High School whom representation of eight regencies and one municipality in Bali were not expected by the Bali Local Government. The total score that achieved TOEIC 2012 under the national standard.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">From the result obtained it in TOEIC 2012 interesting to examine the extend to which the ability of real 75 English Teacher and why it is so lacking.</span></p>

<h1><a name="bookmark11"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark12"></a>Factors Influences The Result of TOEIC</span></h1>
<p><span class="font1">Two courses of low value TOEIC 2012 result.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">TOEIC 2012 results which followed by as English teacher vocational High School from eight regencies and one municipality representatives in Bali are less satisfactory to all sides of Balinese Government generally and TOIEC 2012 participate alone. This is caused two factor that lead to low yield TOEIC score in 2012, namely.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font1">1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Psychology factor</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">Psychology factor is their readiness mentally and fisk in national examination.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font1">2. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Non psychology factor</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">Non psychology factor is educational background they achieved today.</span></p>
<h1><a name="bookmark13"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark14"></a>The Way to Increase the Result of TOEIC</span></h1>
<p><span class="font1">The TOEIC speaking and writing tests measure the proficiency of listening and reading skills indirectly</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Language skill level are related because of the nature of language learning; the skills of listening and speaking or reading and writing are often used together. For most people, there is quite a strong correlation among the four language skills. Over time, however, if the english teacher of vocational high school as examinee practice some skills for example, reading and</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">listening much more often than they practice others, then the correlation may change. While the TOEIC listening and reading tests are indirect measures of speaking and writing proficiency, the TOEIC speaking and writing tests are more direct measures of productive skills.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Benefit of TOEIC</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font1">1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Benchmarking Tool</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">Verifies current level of English proficiency, Identifies strengths and weakness and supports tosed learning goals and monitor progress in English.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font1">2. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Serves Multiple Purposes</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">Provides English language credentials global employers needs and supports migration and proves the English skills required to succed in workplace.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font1">3. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Reliable, fair and valid</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">Measures everyday English skill needed in the global workplace, uses real world examples of spoken and written English collected from workplace around the world, and Measure the intended attributes only.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font1">4. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Convenience and flexibility</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">Test can be administered at a business location at choice and online and separation of listening and reading from speaking and writing offers more flexibility.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font1">5. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Highly Accurate</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">Offers quantifiable standard of performance with consistent and accurate scoring, grounded with principle research in preparing and testing then answers are distributed among multiple raters to avoid discrimination and improve quality.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">International Standard</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">TOEIC is the world is leading test of English language proficiency in a workplace context and It is an English language test proficiency test for people whose native language is not English.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Objective and Reliable</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Provides accurate result all the time, gives an objective assessment of English language proficiency and offers a quantifiable standard of performance.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Practical Test</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">It measures the everyday English of people working in an international environment.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Developed to meet the needs of the working world</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Test questions are developed from samples of spoken and written language collected from various countries around the world where English is used in the workplace.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Study for TOEIC</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">TOEIC scores are accepted as proof of English ability by many multinational corporations world wide.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">TOEIC is an English language exam token by people who want to use English in their job.</span></p>
