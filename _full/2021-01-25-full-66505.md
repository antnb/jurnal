The results of the writing of this journal show that the legal protection of songwriters whose works are freely downloaded via internet sites is carried out administratively"
  - "through civil lawsuits and criminal law instruments. Legal steps taken to protect songwriters whose works are freely downloaded via the internet are carried out in various ways"
