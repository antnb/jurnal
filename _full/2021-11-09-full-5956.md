<h1><a name="bookmark0"></a><span class="font0" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark1"></a>ABSTRAK</span></h1>
<p><span class="font0">Penelitian ini menjelaskan tentang analisis bahasa kiasan dalam lirik lagu. Tujuan utama dari penelitian ini adalah mengetahui jenis bahasa kiasan yang digunakan dalam lirik lagu dan tema yg terkandung dalam lirik-lirik tersebut.</span></p>
<p><span class="font0">Tiga lagu yang terpilih sebagai data seperti “</span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">‘Ring Of Fire’</span><span class="font0">, </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">‘Ghost Town Blues’</span><span class="font0">, dan </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">‘Machine Gun Blues’.</span><span class="font0"> Teori utama yang digunakan dalam penelitian ini adalah teori bahasa kiasan yg dikemukakan oleh Knickerbocker dan Reninger. Analisis ini juga didukung oleh teori makna yang dikemukakan oleh Smith.</span></p>
<p><span class="font0">Penemuan ini menunjukan ada tujuh jenis dari bahasa kiasan yang ditemukan yaitu, </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">namely, allusion, simile, hyperbole, personification, synecdoche, metaphor</span><span class="font0"> dan </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">metonymy.</span><span class="font0"> Kegunaan dari bahasa kiasan dalam lirik lagu ini berperan penting, ini menunjukan intensitas expresi yang dalam dan memiliki cita rasa yang spesial bagi seseorang yang mendengar lagu-lagu tersebut.</span></p>
<p><span class="font0">Kata kunci : </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">Kiasan, Exspresi,Tema</span></p>

<h1><a name="bookmark8"></a><span class="font0" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark9"></a>4. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Research Method</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font0">The research method in this study is classified into three parts, they are: data source, method and technique of collecting data, and also method and technique of analyzing data.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark10"></a><span class="font0" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark11"></a>4.1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Data Source</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font0">The data were taken from song lyrics of the one American Punk Rock Band, namely Social Distortion. There were three songs analyzed, they are “</span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">Ring of Fire”</span><span class="font0">, </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">“Ghost Town Blues”</span><span class="font0">, </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">and “Machine Gun Blues”. </span><span class="font0">These songs were released on March 1990 in their single. All of the songs were written by Mike Ness, the lead singer of the band. The song lyrics will be chosen as the data because they contain a lot of figurative languages.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark12"></a><span class="font0" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark13"></a>4.2. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Method and Technique of Collecting Data</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font0">Firstly, the data were be collected by did observation, after choosing the song lyrics as the data source, they were be read thoroughly, and then followed by classifying the lyrics or the words found in those songs to have the classification of figurative language in the song lyrics themselves, so it is found how many figurative language are used.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark14"></a><span class="font0" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark15"></a>4.3. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Method and Technique of Analyzing Data</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font0">The data was analyzed based on the theory of figurative language proposed by Knickerbocker &amp;&nbsp;Reninger (1963). The song lyrics in “</span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">Ring of Fire”</span><span class="font0">, </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">“Ghost Town Blues”</span><span class="font0">, </span><span class="font0" style="font-style:italic;">and “Machine Gun Blues”</span><span class="font0"> were examined to determine the figurative language found in it. Then, they were classified based on their types. It was done to facilitate in appropriate interpretation of the figurative language put in those song lyrics. In order to understand the song lyrics, it is important to read the lyric carefully, especially to find out the theme.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark16"></a><span class="font0" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark17"></a>5. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Analysis</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font0">Hyperbole is a statement which is emphasized by overstatement or an exaggeration used for special effect (Knickerbocker, 1963:367). The use of hyperbole in this song is very dominant; it can be seen in the following line:</span></p>
<p><span class="font0">‘</span><span class="font0" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">Love is burning thing’</span><span class="font0"> (1:1)</span></p>
<p><span class="font0">It seems that the use of hyperbole is present in this line. The use of hyperbole, exist in the first paragraph line one. It is shown clearly in the line </span><span class="font0" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">‘Love is burning thing’</span><span class="font0">.</span></p>
<p><span class="font0">It is impossible if love can burn, actually love is perceived. It is an experience felt by a person for another person. In the lyric above, the writer wants to convey that his love is deeply and strong, it is only addressed to one person.</span></p>
<p><span class="font0" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">‘and it makes the fiery ring’</span><span class="font0"> (1:2)</span></p>
<p><span class="font0">This lyric exists in the first paragraph line two. The words </span><span class="font0" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">“it”</span><span class="font0"> and </span><span class="font0" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">‘the fiery ring’</span><span class="font0"> are shown the use of hyperbole, it is an overstatement. In this case, the word </span><span class="font0" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">“it”</span><span class="font0"> is addressed to </span><span class="font0" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">“Love” </span><span class="font0">it is impossible if love can make a fiery ring. Actually, as we know love is an experience felt by a person for another person. The writer wants to convey that love makes you up on the inside and takes control of your life. If you are not careful with it you will hurt and break. It is a strong feeling of affection</span></p>
<p><span class="font0" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">‘I fell down into a ring of fire’</span><span class="font0"> (1:4)</span></p>
<p><span class="font0">In the line above there is in the first paragraph line four. The words </span><span class="font0" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">‘ring of fire’</span><span class="font0"> are shown the use of hyperbole; it is an overstatement because it is very impossible if people fall down into the fire’s ring. Actually, they will die if they fell into the fire. In this case, the writer wants to convey that they feel his love is really strong and bound only with one person. Even it will make him desprate, broken and numb but his love will be more and more.</span></p>

<h1><a name="bookmark18"></a><span class="font0" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark19"></a>6. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Conclusion</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font0">Having analyzed the figurative language in the song album, there are seven types of figurative language found in the lyrics namely, hyperbole, personification, simile, metonymy, synecdoche, metaphor, and allusion. Every song has different types of figure</span></p>
