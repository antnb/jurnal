This study aims to examine the factors that influence the intention to use Blockchain by auditors working at audit firm in Bali The approach taken is based on a combination of the UTAUT and SQBT models Performance Expectancy Effort Expectancy SocioCultural Factors and Facilitating Conditions are determined as enabler factors that support the intention to use Blockchain The inhibiting factors were adapted from SQBT namely Uncertainty Loss Aversion Sunk Cost
