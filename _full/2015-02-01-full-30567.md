A research has been conducted to determine the variability of the albedo in Bali using MODIS BRDF MCD43A1 satellite data started from 2005 until 2012 The data used in this study is MODIS BRDF MCD43A1 data and MODIS Aerosol Optical Depth data From MODIS BRDF MCD43A1 data black sky and white sky albedo were calculated The results of black sky and white sky albedo combined together and with MODISAerosol Optical Depth data to find the blue sky albedo actual albedo
