This study was conducted to determine the effect of drying temperature and time on the characteristics of blue pea flower tea and obtain drying temperature and time that produce blue pea flower tea with the best characteristics The experimental design used was a factorial Randomize Block Design consisting of 2 factors the first factor was drying temperature namely 50oC 60oC and 70oC and the second factor was drying time namely 3 hours 35 hours and 4 hours
