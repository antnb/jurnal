This research was conducted to determine effect of carrot on the characteristics ledok and the proper concentration of a carrot right to produce ledok with the best characteristics The research method was use a randomized block design analysis with 5 treatment of the concentration carrot additions of 0 10 20 30 and 40 Each treatment was repeated three times to obtain 15 experimental units Data were analyzed by analysis of variance followed by Duncan test
