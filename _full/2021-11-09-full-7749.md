<h1><a name="bookmark0"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark1"></a>ABSTRAK</span></h1>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Jurnal ini berjudul “The Compliments and The Compliment Responses Used by The Main Characters in When Harry Met Sally Movie”. Tujuan dari penelitian ini adalah untuk mengidentifikasi kategori pujian yang digunakan oleh Harry dan Sally dan untuk mengklasifikasikan jenis-jenis respon pujian yang digunakan oleh Harry dan Sally dalam film romantic komedi yang berjudul When Harry Met Sally. Teori yang digunakan dalam penelitian ini adalah teori Herbert (1989) dalam jurnalnya yang berjudul The Ethnography of English Compliments and Compliment Responses: A Contrastive Sketch dan teori Wolfson (1983) dalam jurnalnya yang berjudul An Empirically Based Analysis of Complimenting in American English. Metode yang digunakan dalam menganalisis penelitian ini adalah metode kualitatif. Pembahasan data di awali dengan menonton film When Harry Met Sally, memberi tanda pada pujian dan respon pujian yang digunakan oleh Harry dan Sally dalam percakapan mereka. Hasil dari pembahasan ini menunjukan bahwa Harry dan Sally menggunakan pujian untuk tujuan yang berbeda. Harry menggunakan pujian sebagai strategi untuk mencari perhatian Sally dan Sally menggunakan pujian hanya untuk menjaga persahabatan.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Kata kunci: compliments, compliment responses, gender.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark2"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark3"></a>1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Background</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">Sociolinguistics is the study of language in relation to society.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Sociolinguists are interested in explaining why people speak differently in different social contexts. And the effect of social factors such as social distance, social status, age, gender, class. Language can be considered as a medium for</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">transmitting a message, information, or idea from speaker to hearer. One of the utterances that people often use in conversation through language is compliment and compliment response. Compliment is often found in daily conversation within people in the society. People give compliment to the others in order to establish a good social relationship. In a compliment, there will be compliment response from listener to speaker.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Compliment response is a verbal acknowledgement that the recipients of the compliment hear and is reacted to the compliment. This reaction may differ from one person to another or, more importantly, from one society to another (tripod.com).</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">The reason why the topic was chosen because in this paper, we can discover how the expressions of the main characters in </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">When Harry Met Sally </span><span class="font1">movie use compliment and compliment response in their dialogues and also we can know the effect of gender difference in giving compliment by the main characters in </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">When Harry Met Sally</span><span class="font1"> movie.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark4"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark5"></a>2. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Problems of the Study</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">Based on the background above, the problems of this study are:</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font1">a) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What categories of compliment are used by Harry and Sally in their dialogues in </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">When Harry Met Sally</span><span class="font1">?</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font1">b) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What types of compliment responses are used by Harry and Sally in their dialogues in </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">When Harry Met Sally</span><span class="font1">?</span></p></li></ul>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark6"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark7"></a>3. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Aims of the Study</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">Related to the problems of the study, the aims of this study are to identify the categories of compliment used by Harry and Sally and to classify the types of compliment response used by Harry and Sally in </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">When Harry Met Sally Movie</span><span class="font1">.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark8"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark9"></a>4. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Research Method</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">In this study, the research method was classified into three parts; they are data source, method and technique of collecting data, and method and technique of analyzing data.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark10"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark11"></a>4.1 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Data Source</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">The data was taken from </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">When Harry Met Sally</span><span class="font1"> movie. This movie is chosen because it is like reflection about human life and in movie we can see directly how the main characters in </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">When Harry Met Sally</span><span class="font1"> use compliment and compliment response. The data was also taken from the movie script in order to get data clearly, especially in conversation.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark12"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark13"></a>4.2 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Method and Technique of Collecting Data</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">The method that was used to collect the data in this research is qualitative method. The qualitative research may use the interview, documentation, and questionnaires. This study used the documentation because the documentation is a method is used for analyzing the data in social research method. In detail, movie included in the documentation matters.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark14"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark15"></a>4.3 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Method and Technique of Analyzing Data</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">There are three techniques to analyze the data. Firstly, watching the When Harry Met Sally movie and reading the movie script then underline the compliment and the compliment response that occur in main characters utterances. Secondly, identify and quote the compliment based on the categories of compliment proposed by Wolfson’s theory (1983). Thirdly, classify the compliment response based on types of compliment response by Herbert’s theory (1989).</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">5. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Analysis of The Compliments and The Compliment Responses Used by The Main Characters in </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">When Harry Met Sally Movie.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;">
<li>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">5.1 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Compliment Categories</span></p></li></ul></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">Conversation 1</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Sally: (talking to herself) Ok, so fifteen percent of my share is ninety... six ninety. This leaves seven. (To Harry) What? Do I have something on my face?</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Harry: </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;text-decoration:underline;">You're a very attractive person.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Sally: Thank you.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Harry: Amanda never said how attractive you were.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">Analysis: </span><span class="font1">One of the </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Compliments on Personal Trait</span><span class="font1"> can be seen in the conversation mentioned above. In this conversation, Harry praised Sally. Harry said “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">you’re a very attractive person”</span><span class="font1">. It was a </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Compliment on Personal Trait, </span><span class="font1">because the word “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">attractive</span><span class="font1">” show about someone’s personality.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">Conversation 2</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Sally: We are just going to be friends, ok?</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Harry: yeaa, </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;text-decoration:underline;">you are good friend!</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Sally: </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">(no response)</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">Analysis: </span><span class="font1">Based on the above conversation, there was a compliment who uttered by Harry to Sally. Harry said “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">you are good friend”.</span><span class="font1"> It was </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Compliment on Personal Trait,</span><span class="font1"> because on the movie seen Sally can made Harry felt comfort when he was close to Sally because of her good personality. Because Harry felt comfort, he praised Sally by saying “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">you are good friend</span><span class="font1">”.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">5.2 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Compliment Response Types</span></p></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">Conversation 3</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Harry: Thanks for the ride.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Sally: Yeah, it was interesting.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Harry</span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">: </span><span class="font1">It was nice knowing you.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Sally</span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">: </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;text-decoration:underline;">Yeah.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">(They shake hands)</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">Analysis: </span><span class="font1">After Sally heard compliment by Harry, Sally smiled to Harry and she responded Harry’s compliment by saying “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">yeah</span><span class="font1">”. This response showed acceptance. So that this response called </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Appreciation Token.</span><span class="font1"> It was called</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Appreciation Token</span><span class="font1"> because Sally received Harry’s compliment by saying “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">yeah</span><span class="font1">” and smiled to him.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">Conversation 4</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Harry: You were a good friend of umm...</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Sally: </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;text-decoration:underline;">Really?</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Harry: of course, you are.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">Analysis: </span><span class="font1">From the compliment response type above, it showed </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Questioning type. </span><span class="font1">After accepted compliment from Harry, Sally was not sure about Harry’s compliment, so that she said “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Really</span><span class="font1">”. It was </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">questioning</span><span class="font1">, because by this word showed that the addressee questions the sincerity or the appropriateness of the compliment to the speaker.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark16"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark17"></a>6. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Conclusion</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">From the findings and the analysis, some conclusions can be drawn from this movie. First, it can be said that Harry made more compliments than Sally. Of all the categories of compliments by Wolfson (1983), Harry praised Sally more often by using compliments on personal trait when they were talking or arguing with each other. Secondly, Harry more often used compliments, so automatically Sally more often responded than Harry. In her responding compliment, Sally more often produced appreciation token to respond to Harry's compliment because Sally wanted to make a good relationship to Harry (just as friends). Thirdly, in this movie, gender differences did not influence the ways used in expressing compliments between Harry and Sally. However, in this current study, women more often receive praise not because as the basis of gender but because of Harry has a special feeling to Sally and uses a compliment to attract the attention of Sally.</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">Furthermore, Harry and Sally used compliment for different purposes. Harry used compliments as a strategy to get closer to Sally and to attract Sally' special attention because Harry loved Sally, and Sally used compliments only to express admiration and also to maintain the friendship between them.</span></p>
