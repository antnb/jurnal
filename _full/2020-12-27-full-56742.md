Durian peel Durio zibethinus Murr is a plant that can be used as a treatment Durian skin contains secondary metabolites namely flavonoids which have antibacterial activity The bacteria that cause acne are Propionibacterium acnes and Staphylococcus aureus The extract was obtained by maceration using 70 ethanol solvent The ethanol extract of durian peel obtained was made in 3 series of concentrations of 10 15 and 25 then tested on propionibacterium acnes
