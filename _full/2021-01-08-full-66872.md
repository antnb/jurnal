It has been investigated the effect of slice thickness on image quality with the Contrast to Noise Ratio CNR parameter method using a Toshiba TOS phantom The variations in the slice thickness used were 1 2 3 4 6 and 8 mm The Xray tube voltage and the Xray current time used were 80 kV and 100 mAs which following the patient head examination protocol The analysis of the effect of slice thickness on CNR values is determined with a simple regression test
