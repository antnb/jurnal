ABSTRACT This study aims to examine the form of legal protection for cinematographic works (films) according to Law Number 28 of 2014 concerning Copyright and to analyze legal remedies can be taken by creators for cinematographic works that are used without permission by others through Youtube. In writing this scientific paper used the normative legal method with the Copyright Act as the object of study. This study indicated that legal protection of cinematographic works that are imitated (plagiarism) without permission are regulated in Article 74 UUHC and Article 96.
