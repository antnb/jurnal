The purpose of this study is to determine whether the art Barong Nong-Nong Kling art is included as an object of protection of Traditional Cultural Expressions in Intellectual Property Law and legal protection of Barong Nong-Nong Kling Art as Traditional Cultural Expressions under the Copyright Act. This study uses normative juridical law research methods statutory and comparative approach. The results of the study show that the art of Barong Nong-Nong Kling is included as an object of protection for traditional cultural expressions in Intellectual Property Law"
