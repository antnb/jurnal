This study aims to determine the effect of using types of oil in rations on carcass pieces of male Bali ducks This research was conducted in Dajan Peken Village Tabanan for 10 weeks The research design used was a completely randomized design CRD consisting of 5 treatments and 4 replications and each replication contained 2 male Balinese ducks with a weight range of 2231936 g The treatment consisted of R0  use of 0 oil in the ration R1  use of 3 palm oil in the ration R2  use of 3 fish oil in the ration R3  use of 3 used cooking oil in the ratio"
