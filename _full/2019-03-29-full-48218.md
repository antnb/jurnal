This study aims to determine the effect ratio of semolina and black rice flour to the characteristics of wet fettucine paste and to know the right ratio for semolina and black rice flour to produce wet fettucine paste with the best characteristics This study used a Completely Randomized Design RAL with a comparison treatment of semolina and black rice flour  100  0 90  10 80  20 70  30 60  40 and 50  50 The treatment was repeated 3 times
