Bali cattle are native Indonesian cattle as a result of the longrunning domestication of wild banteng and are one of the germplasm that has the potential to be developed This study aims to determine the diversity and correlation of body width dimensions of bali cattle The samples used were 25 cows that were mature and had given birth The data obtained were analyzed by descriptive analysis and biplot analysis and then presented in the form of tables and graphs
