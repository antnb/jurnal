In turmeric rhizome there are active compounds in the form of flavonoids which act as antioxidants by increasing the expression of miR-25 so that it can reduce the amount of NOX4. This study aims to prove the role of flavonoid compounds from turmeric ethanol extract on hypercholesterolemic mice models. This study is an in vivo experimental study using a post-test only with control group design.
