The government which has the right to supervise and impose sanctions on the existence of such business violations is KPPU as an independent institution that is given attributive authority in the Business Competition Law as referred to in Article 36 to impose sanctions in Article 47 such as administrative sanctions and articles. 48 as the principal punishment imposed by the business actor. in this case an individual or legal entity domiciled or conducting business activities in the jurisdiction of the Unitary State of the Republic of Indonesia can be followed up on based on the Law on Business Competition.
