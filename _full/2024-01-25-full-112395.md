This research aims to identify building types in landslide hazard areas and to determine the distribution of buildings in landslide hazard areas in Candikuning Village The method used is detailed survey per building by assessing buildings in landslide hazard area by direct field survey Field results show that building types in the hazard area are divided into 3 classes namely high hazard class medium hazard class and low hazard class with 7 building categories
