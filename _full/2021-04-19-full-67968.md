  - "Masyarakat Adat ABSTRACT The purpose of this research is to understand the position of the state as the holder of the Copyright for Traditional Cultural Expressions (EBT) in Law No. 28 of 2014 concerning Copyright and to find out the status of indigenous peoples as executors in the conservation of EBT from a Copyright perspective. The research method used in this study is a normative legal research method with a statutory and conceptual approach. The results show that the state's position as the copyright holder of traditional cultural expressions is contained in the provisions of Article 38 and Article 39 of the UUHC which emphasize that the state's position is as the holder of authorization for EBT.
