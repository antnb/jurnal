The 2020 pandemic affected the economic sector including banking sector especially banks in the BUKU 1 category This study aims to identify internal factors that affect bank profitability between normal conditions in 2019 and conditions when economic growth experienced a contraction due to the pandemic in 2020 This study is based on the theory of AssetLiability Management ALM and uses multiple linear regression method with ROA as a measure of profitability
