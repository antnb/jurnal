This study aims to determine the existence of market reaction to the Announcement of the Election Results of the Cabinet Work Volume II of the President of Indonesia in 2019 This research is an event study with event window for 7 days The study was conducted on companies listed in the LQ45 Index for the period AugustJanuary 2020 The population of this study were 45 companies listed in the LQ45 index The sampling method used is the purposive sampling method
