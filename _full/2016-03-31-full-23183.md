The purpose of this study was to determine functional and chemical properties of red bean Phaseolus vulgaris L sprouts flour The design used was Completely Randomized Design with three replications The results showed that the germination of red beans for 2 days did not affect the functional properties of flour Functional properties of red bean sprouts flour are water absorption capacity of 14535 oil absorption capacity of 8984 swelling power of 306 g
