One of the problems that occurs is that the goods that arrive are not in accordance with what was previously agreed between the business actor and buyer. The problem studied in this study is how is the legal protection for buyers if the product purchased is not in accordance with the storefront in the sale and purchase agreement through e-commerce? and "What is the responsibility of the business actor for the loss of consumers who get goods that do not match the storefront?"
