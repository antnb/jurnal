At least 30 cetaceans live in Indonesia especially in the eastern waters of Indonesia consisting of whales and dolphins Cetaceans play an important role as a key species in determining the health of marine ecosystems This study aims to determine the frequency of appearance behavior and diversity of cetaceans along Labuan Bajo Flores and East Nusa Tenggara The study was conducted in OctoberNovember 2021 with four weeks to collect field data and interviews
