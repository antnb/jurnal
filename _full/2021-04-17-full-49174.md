This study aims to find out whether the sort card media can improve speaking skills in learning to tell the experience of students of class X3 of SMAN 2 Lasalimu Selatan This research was compiled based on the Classroom Action Research CAR design which was divided into two cycles with four stages per cycle namely planning action observation and reflection The first cycle was held from 9 to 14 January 2017 The second cycle was held on January 16 to 21 2017
