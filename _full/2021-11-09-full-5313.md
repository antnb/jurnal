<p><span class="font0">1</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">FORM, FUNCTION, AND MEANING OF “OVER” AND “ABOVE” IN</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">TRANSLATION</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">By</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">MADE BAYU SEGARA</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">ENGLISH DEPARTMENT</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">FACULTY OF LETTERS UDAYANA UNIVERSITY</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">ABSTRAK</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Kata </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">Over </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">dan </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">above </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">merupakan kata dalam bahasa inggris yang lebih dikenal sebagai preposisi. Padahal sesuai teori yang ada kedua kata tersebut memiliki berbagai variasi dalam bentuk, fungsi dan makna dalam kalimat. </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">Over </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">dan </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">above </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">memiliki beberapa variasi bentuk dalam kalimat seperti menjadi kata sifat, preposisi, kata keterangan predikat, dan ungkapan kata kerja. </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">Over </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">dan </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">above </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">memiliki 4 variasi fungsi seperti tempat, waktu, alat, dan penyebab. </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">Over </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">dan </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">above </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">juga memiliki berbagai makna dalam terjemahan sebagai contoh yang ada dalam novel “Percy Jackson The Olympians, The Battle of Labyrinth” dan terjemahannya ditemukan variasi makna dari kata </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">over </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">seperti : ke, di atas, di, atas,</span><span class="font1"> dan </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">setelah. Dalam novel tersebut juga ditemukan hanya satu variasi makna dari kata </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">above </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">seperti: di atas</span><span class="font1">. </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Dalam penelitian ini, Kata </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">over </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">dan </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">above </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">dianalisis dalam aspek bentuk, fungsi, dan makna sesuai teori Larson (1998) dan Quirk et.al (1985).</span></p>
<p><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Kata kunci: bentuk, fungsi, makna, dan preposisi</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li><a name="caption1"></a>
<h1><a name="bookmark0"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark1"></a>1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Background of the Study</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">Starting from a need that translating skill is one important aspect in language. The English prepositions </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">over </span><span class="font1">and </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">above </span><span class="font1">have similarities in form, function, and meaning. Form, Function, and Meaning are three important factors in translation. The translator must discover the meaning of the source language and use the target language form that expresses this meaning in a natural way</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">.</span><span class="font1"> In translation, meaning is more important than form. Because of that, many problems come up in translating a text. The translator should choose the most appropriate meaning of the key words namely, </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">over </span><span class="font1">and </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">above </span><span class="font1">which have more than one</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">meanings. This study analyzed two English words </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">over </span><span class="font1">and </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">above </span><span class="font1">found in the novel entitled “Percy Jackson The Olympians, The Battle of Labyrinth”.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark2"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark3"></a>2. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Problems of the Study</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">Based on the background above, three problems are discussed in this study. They are:</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font1">1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What are the forms and meaning of “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">over”</span><span class="font1"> and “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">above”</span><span class="font1"> in the novel?</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font1">2. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;What are the functions of “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">over”</span><span class="font1"> and “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">above”</span><span class="font1"> in the novel?</span></p></li>
<li>
<h1><a name="bookmark4"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark5"></a>3. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Aims of the Study</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">The aims of this study are:</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<p><span class="font1">1. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To classify the forms and meaning of “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">over”</span><span class="font1"> and “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">above”</span><span class="font1"> in the novel.</span></p></li>
<li>
<p><span class="font1">2. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;To describe the functions of “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">over”</span><span class="font1"> and “</span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">above”</span><span class="font1"> in the novel.</span></p></li></ul>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark6"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark7"></a>4. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Research Method</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">To carry out a scientific research, the research method must be followed. The research method in this study can be explained in 3 main categories of points, data source, method of collecting data, and data analysis.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark8"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark9"></a>4.1.1 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Data Source</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">The data source of this study was taken from novel “Percy Jackson The Olympians, The Battle of Labyrinth”. This study analyzed two English words </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">over</span><span class="font1"> and </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">above</span><span class="font1"> that were explained before in the background. This source was used because words </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">over</span><span class="font1"> and </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">above</span><span class="font1"> have similarities in form and function that made this study interesting.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark10"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark11"></a>4.1.2 &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Method and Technique of Collecting Data</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">In collecting data, library research was applied. The library research was used to obtain information on translation theories, the concepts of form and function. It is relevant to be used in analyzing form and function. The data were collected from all text in novel. Then data were selected which ones are appropriate for the study.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark12"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark13"></a>4.2 Method and Technique of Analyzing Data</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">The data were analyzed based on the theories applied in this study. The theories used are the theory of Translation from Larson (1998) and the theory of Preposition by Quirk et.al (1972).</span></p>
<p><span class="font1">First, the theory from Larson about meaning of translation was used to analyze and find out the meaning and types of translation of the words </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">over</span><span class="font1"> and </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">above</span><span class="font1">. The theory from Hornby proposed by (2005) was used to find out the kinds of form of both words. Next, the functions of </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">over</span><span class="font1"> and </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">above</span><span class="font1"> were analyzed using the theory from Quirk et.al (1972).</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>
<h1><a name="bookmark14"></a><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"><a name="bookmark15"></a>5. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Analysis of </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">over</span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"> and </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;font-style:italic;">above</span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;"> in Translation</span></h1></li></ul>
<p><span class="font1">In the novel were found some variations of form and meaning of </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">over</span><span class="font1"> and </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">above</span><span class="font1">. Hornby (2005:1079) states that </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">over</span><span class="font1"> is a word that can be adverb, preposition and noun. For special uses of </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">over</span><span class="font1"> in phrasal verbs, look at the entries for the verbs. For example </span><span class="font1" style="font-weight:bold;">take something over </span><span class="font1">is in the phrasal verb section of taking.</span></p>
<ul style="list-style:none;"><li>

<p><span class="font1">Hornby states that </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">over</span><span class="font1"> can become 10 adverbs and 14 prepositions. Based on the data found there are form of </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">over</span><span class="font1"> as 8 kinds of preposition, 2 kinds of adverb, 1 adjective and 6 phrasal verbs. Hornby (2005:4) also states that </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">above </span><span class="font1">can become 5 kinds of prepositions and 3 adverbs. </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">Above</span><span class="font1"> becomes only preposition 1 that has meaning: somebody/something that is at a higher place of position than another. It shows the function of </span><span class="font1" style="font-style:italic;">above</span><span class="font1"> as place that found in the novel.</span></p>
