Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus T2DM is a chronic disease caused by an increase in blood sugar levels in the blood which will lead complication Peripheral neuropathy is one of the most common chronic complication in T2DM patients Chronic hyperglycemia in T2DM causes damage to the peripheral nerves namely autonomic sensory and motor nerves and then triggers neuropathy The purpose of this study was to determine the level of peripheral neuropathy in type 2 diabetes
