## Abstract:
"The purpose of this research is to test the influence of intangible asset value and research &amp; development expenditure on corporate market value and financial performance of manufacturing companies which were listed on Indonesia Stock Exchange (IDX) during 2010-2013. Samples were determined using purposive sampling method and consisting of 40 companies with 155 observations. The data in this study was collected through non-partisipan observation method by downloading data from IDX. The testing of hypotheses in this research used multiple linear regression. The result shows that intangible assets value and research &amp; development expenditure influence positively and significantly on corporate market value and financial performance."
