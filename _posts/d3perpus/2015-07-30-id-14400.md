## Abstract:
"ABSTRACT The purpose of the research is to know about school library SD No.2 Kutuh Kuta Selatan in supporting teaching and learning program.In this research used qualitative data. The data are collected by interview, observation and documentation. The sampling method which was used in this research is purposive sampling method. Purposive sampling method is sampling technique based on certain consideration, where informants were used as sampel considered most knowledgeable about the object to be observed.The reseult was the of this research show that school library SD No.2 Kutuh Kuta Selatan in supporting teaching and learning program are very helpful. Because the library of SD No.2 Kutuh has been provided many collections of books and sufficient facilities in supporting the teaching and learning program, but some teachers have not used the school library in supporting teaching and learning program. Keywords : school library, teaching and learning"
