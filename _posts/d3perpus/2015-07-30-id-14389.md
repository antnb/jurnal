## Abstract:
"ABSTRACK The purpose of this research is the effort in the student reading interest at the library of faculty of letter and culture Udayana university. The university library is the library which is located suchas University, Academy, Faculty, Institute or higher education, as a means of supporting the teaching and learning academic activity in order to carry out the three Dutises of University. This study discussed the University library at Faculty of Letter and Culture Udayana University. This research used a descriptive qualitative method is a method that gives a real picture, circumstance or situation, by collecting the necessary data and then presented in accordance with the writing guidelines. The results of this research its expression of the effort done by librarians in improving of student reading interest at Faculty of Letter and Culture Udayana University. Keywords: University Library, Reading Interest"
