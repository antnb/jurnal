## Abstract:
"ABSTRACT The aim ofthis study is to determine. the application of library automation systems in processing library materials in the public library of the Library and Archives Office at Jembrana Regency using descriptive research methods with a qualitative approach. Sources.of data in this study are textbooks, journals, research.results, field observations.and interviews. Informants in this study are three informans, the head of the library, librarians and library users. The results of this.study indicated that the existence of a library automation system was very helpful for librarians in the public library of the Department of Library and Archives at Jembrana Regency in processing library materials and was also able to assist library users in information retrieval. In processing public library materials, the Department of Library and Archives Office of Jembrana Regency was quite optimal. Keywords: Library Automation, Integrated Library System (INLIS Late), Processing of Library Materials"
