## Abstract:
"ABSTRACT The collection in the library SMAN 1 Kintamani a good enough, already support academicians in the field of education, but the new books to be renewed and old books in the library need maximum care because it is still in need by librarian as a means of information and references. The results of this study, found that library visits (78 respondents = 31.5%) the length of time to the library (187 respondents = 75.4%), use of the collection (110 respondents = 44.4%), how to take advantage of the collection (133 respondents = 53.7%), the collection is available (122 respondents = 49.1%), which is already a new collection (up to date) (147 respondents = 59.3%), the collection helps in learning (140 respondents = 56.5%), the facility is already fair (132 respondents = 53.3%), librarian help to find collections (128 respondents = 51.6%), librarians provide guidance (124 respondents = 50%). Keywords: Library wage, School Library"
