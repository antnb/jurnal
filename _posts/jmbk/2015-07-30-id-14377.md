## Abstract:
"Thi s study aims to analyze the moderating role of work experience on the effect of work stress. The study wasconducted at the Bali Dynasty Resort Kuta with a sample of 84 people, selected by proportioned stratifiedrandom sampl ing method. Data were analyzed using Moderated Regression Analysis (MRA).
