## Abstract:
"A sample of 50 famele respondents in the age group 18-50 years in the city of Denpasar, shows that dominant factors considered as causes for extra-marital affairs are disharmony between the spouses, as well as economic, social and psychological factors. More than 50% of the respondents condemn their spouse for having an affair, but they prefer to forgive their patner instead of seeking a divorce."
