## Abstract:
"Community Epidemic and Pandemic Preparedness Program (CP3) is a program conducted by PMI Kabupaten Tabanan to preventive measure implemented through communication to lessen the case of dengue fever in Belalang village. The residents become more comprehend on dengue fever, including the definition, the transmission, symptoms, high-risk group, prevention, and the handling. This also influences the decreasing of dengue fever cases in Belalang village in 2019-2020, namely 40%. Therefore, through this study, the author would like to describe the communication strategy used by PMI Kabupaten Tabanan on CP3 in Belalang village. The author used the Lasswell communication model and descriptive- qualitative method in the present study.
