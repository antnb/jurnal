## Abstract:
"The social problems with students in Japan such as ijime, futoukou, andthe others problem are often used as a theme in literature works like novels orcomics. Michikusa Kyoushitsu comic is one of Japanese literature works whichhas the theme of student social problems in Japan. Michikusa Kyoushitsu comicalso tells about free school that has became one of the solutions in social problemstudents in Japan. According to the result of analysis, it can be known about freeschool, it’s background of free school, education system of free school, and theother event which happening at free school, and the society opinion about freeschool in Japan. It can also be learnt about social interaction of students at freeschool in the relation of miuchi and nakama."
