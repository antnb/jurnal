## Abstract:
"Ngurah Parsua is a poet who care about the environment. Ideas, views, and criticisms ofthe environment conveyed through poetry. As well as rhyme “Potret Pohon Air Mata”contained criticisms of the destruction of the environment today. Diction in his poem canbe said to have high aesthetic value, because it uses poetic language which contains afigure of speech. Ngurah Parsua also uses diction sad like the word ‘tear’ that can befound in each poem. Through word ‘tears’ he depicts the sufferings of this presentenvironment."
