## Abstract:
"This research describes the types and meaning of idiom found inAkutagawa Ryunosuke’s short story Rashoumon and Yabu no Naka. This researchused the descriptive analysis method. The data were analyzed based on type ofidiom theory by Hiroshi Miyaji, lexical meaning theory by Chaer, and contextualtheory by Firth. It has been found 18 idioms as the result of analysis. The type ofidiom from 18 data are 15 data doushi kanyouku, 2 data keiyoushi kanyouku, andone data meishi kanyouku. most of idioms do not have a well-understood lexicalmeaning, where as idiomatical meaning can be understood by considering thecontext of sentence."
