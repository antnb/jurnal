## Abstract:
"The purpose of this study was determined pH and the total plate count (TPC) of bacteria in the rumen of bali cattle. The research material is rumen fluid of male’s bali cattle as many as 17 samples. This research is observational analytic Cross Sectional approach with observations on TPC of bacteria and pH in the rumen. The data obtained are then presented descriptively. Then, the difference in the amount of TPC at a certain pH was analyzed by Independent Sample T- test. The results showed that the rumen pH range of bali cattle was 6.8 to 7 with the average was 6,4 ± 0,1 and the range of TPC numbers was 32 x 103 to 171 x 103 CFU /ml with the average was 74 x 103 ± 47 x 103 CFU/ml. There is no significant difference between the TPC at pH 6.8 and the TPC at pH 7."
