## Abstract:
"Denpasar is the capital city of Bali Province which has grown into a metropolis . To improve capabilities in the areas of funding for the activities of their own regions , both for governance and for public services , the government tried to increase revenue through local taxes . One of the factors that affect revenue source is the growth of GDP . The purpose of this study was to determine the direct effect on GDP and PAD strip Tax , Local Tax direct influence on PAD , and Indirect Effects of GDP to PAD Denpasar through the Regional Tax . The data used in this study is a secondary data sourced from BPS Denpasar , which is then analyzed with path analysis method. Results showed GDP directly affects the Local Taxes and PAD Denpasar , Local Tax directly affect PAD Denpasar , and indirectly affect the GDP of the Regional Tax revenue through Denpasar ."
