## Abstract:
"This study aimed to determine the effect of the amount of production, prices, and investment to Indonesia rubber exports in 1996-2010, either simultaneously or partially. The analysis technique used is regeresi linear regression using the F test and t test .. The data analysis showed that the amount of production, prices and investment simultaneously significant effect on Indonesian rubber exports in 1996-2010. Partially only variable significant amount of production to Indonesia rubber exports in 1996-2010, while the price variable and the investment does not significantly influence the volume of rubber exports, Indonesia in 1996 - 2010."
