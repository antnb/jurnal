## Abstract:
"Red rice bran is a waste of cendana red rice mill which is widely available inTabanan regency, Bali. Red rice bran which produced rarely used as feed products. Thered rice bran has good nutrition such as anthocyanins, phenolics and antioxidant activityrespectively 109.33 mg/100 g rice bran, rice bran 4.38 mg/100 g, and 88.10%.
