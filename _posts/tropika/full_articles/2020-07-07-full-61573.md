This study aims to increase the production of 95weekold Isa Brown chicken eggs fed commercial rations plus oyster shell as a calcium The study was conducted in Pesedahan Village Manggis District Karangasem Regency for 4 weeks The research material used was Isa Brown laying hens aged 95 weeks The research design used was a Completely Randomized Design CRD consisting of 4 treatments namely commercial ration without the addition of calcium oyster shell as a control A commercial ration plus 1 oyster shell B commercial ration plus 2 clam oyster shell"
