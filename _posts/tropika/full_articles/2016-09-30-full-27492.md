This research was carried to determine the weight of commercial carcass pieces of kampung chickens that given fermented ration with probiotic based a papaya leaf extract The design used in this study was a randomized block design RBD with four treatments and four groups as replication so there are 16 experimental units The treatments were A 70 commercial ration  30 corn bran as a control B A ration  8 probiotic based a papaya leaf extract C A ration  12 probiotic based a papaya leaf extract and D A ration  16 probiotic based a papaya leaf"
