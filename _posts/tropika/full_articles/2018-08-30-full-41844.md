This research aims to determine the quality of eggs of chicken placed on wooden box wire box and egg tray carton for 7 days The research was conducted at Poultry Livestock Laboratory of Faculty of Animal Husbandry for 3 week This research used a sample of 90 chicken eggs The design used was Completely Randomized Design CRD with 6 replication and three treatments storage using wooden box wire box and carton egg tray at average temperature 25oC The variables observed on egg quality test were egg weight egg index thick of shell heavy of shell yolk color"
