## Abstract:
"Renewable sources of energy by utilizing water resources, more specifically is PLTMH (Micro-Hydro power plants) to be an excellent choice for potential to be developed in order to overcome the scarcity of energy. For ease in learning about PLTMH then use PLTMH prototype by designing the nozzle position on the corner of 00, 150, 300, 450, 600 and 750 to measurements, using three types of turbine vanes, on the position of the nozzle at an angle of 600 produces round generator and turbine rotation. The position of the nozzle at an angle of 600, a model turbine vanes are recommended to be used is semicircular vanes model because the model in the position and the power-generating turbine vanes most large."
